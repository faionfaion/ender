# Steal a Brainrot Just Broke Roblox: 25 MILLION Players at Once!

Okay friends, you NEED to sit down for this one. Steal a Brainrot just did something no Roblox game has ever done before — it hit **25 million concurrent players**. That means 25 million people were all playing AT THE SAME TIME. My brain is officially rotted just thinking about it.

## Wait, What Even Is Steal a Brainrot?

If you've been living under a rock (a very cozy rock, no judgment), here's the deal. The game was made by a developer named SpyderSammy back in 2025, and it has completely taken over Roblox in 2026.

You build a base, collect goofy little voxel characters called Brainrots (yes, based on those wild Italian Brainrot memes), and then — here's the spicy part — you raid other players' bases to STEAL their stuff. You can team up with friends, form alliances, or betray everyone for the rarest collection. It's chaos. It's beautiful.

## The Numbers Are Unreal

Before Steal a Brainrot, the only game to ever pass 20 million concurrent users was Grow a Garden. Now Steal a Brainrot blew right past that and became the FIRST game in Roblox history to cross 25 million. That's a record nobody saw coming.

Bloomberg even wrote a whole article in March about how the game is fighting off a million copycat games trying to steal its vibe. When Bloomberg writes about your Roblox game, you know you've made it.

## Bruno Mars Showed Up?? Inside the Game??

This is the part that sent me. Back in January 2026, Bruno Mars actually performed a virtual concert INSIDE Steal a Brainrot. He played his new 2026 single "I Just Might" from the album *The Romantic*, plus the classic "Locked Out of Heaven." A real pop star. In a Brainrot game. The crossover nobody asked for but everybody loved.

## The Cursed Event Is Live Right Now

If you log in this week, you'll find the **Cursed Event** happening — there are new rituals to do and rare cursed Brainrots dropping all the time. Perfect time to jump in if you've never tried it. Just maybe don't trust your "alliance" too much. Just saying.

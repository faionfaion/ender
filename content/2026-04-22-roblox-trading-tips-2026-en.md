## First Things First: How Trading Actually Works

Roblox trading only works with Limited and Limited U items — those are the ones with the green banners in your inventory. You also need Roblox Premium (starts at $4.99/month) and a verified account. No Premium, no trading. Sorry!

And here's the golden rule: real trades ALWAYS happen through Roblox's official trade window, and both sides swap at the same time. If someone says "send first, I'll send after" — that's a scam. Every. Single. Time.

## Know Your Values (Don't Get Lowballed!)

Roblox shows you RAP (Recent Average Price), but that number can be tricked by sneaky traders. The community's go-to site is Rolimon's — it's been tracking Roblox trading since 2018, basically the Bloomberg of Robux. Check the Best Price, Available Copies, and the Trade Calculator before any deal.

If you're into Adopt Me!, check Traderie or AdoptMeValues.app. Fun fact: as of March 2026, the Shadow Dragon is still the #1 most valuable pet, with the Bat Dragon right behind it. And guess what? The Blue Dog — the very first pet ever added — is technically only "uncommon" but trades higher than legendaries just because of how rare it is now. Old pets = gold mine!

## The Endangered Egg Hype

The Endangered Egg dropped February 28, 2026, with 10 pets including Blue Whale and Sea Turtle (that one's the rarest). Eggs eventually retire, which means their pets become trading treasures. If you pull a Sea Turtle, please don't give it away for a Common Bunny, I'm begging.

## Stay Safe, Trader Fam

1. "Trust trades" = scam. Always.
2. Never click weird links people DM you — the Beam Link phishing scam can steal your cookies even with 2FA on. Scary stuff.
3. Turn on 2FA right now if you haven't.
4. No "free Robux" site is real. Ever.
5. Roblox staff NEVER message you in-game. If someone claims to be staff, block them.
6. Bookmark www.roblox.com so you never land on a fake login page.

Start small, trade only what you're okay losing, and have FUN with it. Trading is like a mini-game inside Roblox — learn the values, read the charts, and flex those big-brain deals. Catch you in the trade window! 💎

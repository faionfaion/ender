Hey gamers, EnderFaion here — and I gotta tell you, this Easter on Roblox is hitting DIFFERENT. There's no big platform-wide event this year (the last one was The Hatch back in 2025), but guess what? The community picked up the basket and ran with it. We've got over 25 games hosting their own egg hunts right now, and some of them are straight-up incredible.

## Grow a Garden Goes HUGE

Okay, buckle up. Grow a Garden dropped its biggest event EVER, and it's split into three parts — Part 1 went live April 4, and Part 3 just hit on April 18, 2026. That's the first time GaG has done a multi-part event, and it's stacked.

You get a whole new Easter 2026 garden plot, a brand-new event island, and a special currency called Choc Coins. The update added 9 exclusive Easter crops, 10 new pets, and 6 fresh mutations to play with. Wild, right?

My favorite part? The Egg War mini-game runs every 60 minutes. Five Easter Eggs spawn around the map, and players use shovels to tag eggs out of each other's hands (no fighting — it's like tag!). There's also a Golden Egg Shop where prices DOUBLE every time someone buys, starting at 25,000 Choc Coins. Grinding goes crazy.

Oh, and find the NPC named Jim — hand him 50 plants and he'll drop Easter Seed Packs on you. Super worth it.

## Brookhaven RP's 4-Tier Hunt

Brookhaven's event already wrapped up on April 3, but if you caught it, you know it was a whole vibe. They split the egg hunt into four difficulty tiers: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Extreme.

Clearing Extreme mode got you 1,475 Bunny Tokens — and Hard mode paid out 580. You could spend those on a Spring Shuffler, an Easter Truck, or even an Easter House for your roleplay setup. Extreme mode hid 25 eggs in secret rooms, underwater spots, and hidden passages. Galaxy-brain stuff.

## More Games Still Running

If you missed some, don't stress — there's still a bunch active:

- Dandy's World runs the longest, all the way through April 24
- Horse Life keeps going until May 2
- Metro Life City RP has been live since March 20
- Escape Waves for Lucky Blocks and Dragon Adventures were short pop-ups (already done)

## What's Next for Roblox

Looking ahead, the PEC26 April Expo showed off tons of new creator projects on April 4, and Roblox is heading to Gamescom Latam in São Paulo from April 30 to May 3. Oh, and RDC26 just got announced — San Jose, September 10-12. Mark your calendars!

So basically, Easter 2026 proved something cool: Roblox doesn't NEED one giant event when the community can build 25 of them. Hop into Grow a Garden before the eggs are gone, and tell me in the comments which game event was your fave!

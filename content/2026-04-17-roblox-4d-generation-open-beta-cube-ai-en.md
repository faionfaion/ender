# Roblox 4D Generation Is HERE — Type Words, Get Flying Dragons!

Okay friends, I need you to sit down because this is HUGE. Roblox just dropped something called **4D Generation** into open beta on February 4, 2026, and it literally changes everything about building. Like, I'm not even exaggerating.

You know how regular 3D models just… sit there? A car that can't drive, a plane that can't fly? Yeah, 4D fixes that. The "4th D" is **interactivity** — meaning the thing you build actually WORKS right out of the box. Wild, right?

## So What Does The "4D" Actually Do?

It's powered by Roblox's new **Cube Foundation Model** (basically a super-smart building AI). You type what you want, and Cube spits out the shape, the textures, AND the physics behind it all at the same time. No scripting, no weird tweaking, no crying over broken wheels.

In the test game **Wish Master** (made by a developer named Laksh), kids were typing stuff like "flying rainbow dragon" and "race car with giant wings" — and the game just… made them. They flew. They drove. They vibed.

## The Numbers Are Bonkers

During early access, players generated **over 160,000 objects**. Let that sink in. And get this — creators who used 4D generation saw a **64% bump in play time** in Wish Master. Turns out when kids can summon their dream car, they wanna stay and play with it. Shocker.

The open beta launched with two starter schemas:
- **Car-5**: a 5-part car (body + 4 wheels that actually roll)
- **Body-1**: any single-mesh object you can dream up

More schemas are coming, so planes, mechs, and who-knows-what-else are on the way.

## Wait, There's MORE

Roblox Studio's AI Assistant also got buffed HARD. It can now build instances, write scripts, shape terrain, set up lighting, and even run a brand-new **Playtest Subagent** that spawns a test character, plays your game, finds bugs, fixes them, and tries again. Solo builders are basically running whole dev teams now.

Oh, and the **Flexible Studio UI** went permanent on January 5, plus **Emissive Masks** are live so your glowy stuff can glow even better.

## Why This Is A Game-Changer

For real though — this is the first time ever that a kid with zero coding experience can type "epic flying castle with lasers" and watch physics-ready gameplay pop into their world. If you've been too scared to start building, NOW is your moment. Go wish something wild into existence. I'll see you in Studio!

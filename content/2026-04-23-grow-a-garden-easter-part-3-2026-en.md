# Grow a Garden Easter Part 3 Is LIVE — 10M Players Can't Be Wrong!

Okay besties, you HAVE to stop what you're doing right now. Grow a Garden just dropped Easter Event Part 3, and this game is literally the biggest Roblox game of ALL TIME. We're talking over **10 MILLION concurrent players**. Ten. Million. That's bigger than anything Roblox has ever seen, and I am losing my mind about it.

If you haven't tried it yet, Grow a Garden is that super chill farming game where you buy seeds, plant them, watch them grow, and sell your crops for coins. Sounds simple? Yeah, until you get pulled in and suddenly it's three hours later and you own a pet bunny.

## The Easter Garden Plot (This Is HUGE)

Part 3 just launched on April 4, 2026, and here's the wild part — for the FIRST TIME EVER, Grow a Garden added a second plot just for event plants. It's called the **Easter Garden Plot**, and you use it only for Easter seeds. No mixing with your regular crops. Pure Easter vibes.

The event currency is called **Choc Coins** (yes, chocolate coins, obviously). You earn them by growing and selling Easter plants, then spend them at the Easter Event Shop for exclusive stuff.

## Egg Hunt Every 15 Minutes 🥚

Okay this is the part I'm most hyped about. Every 15 minutes a new Egg Hunt starts on the map — there are **10 hidden eggs**, and if you find them all, you get random Easter rewards. It's basically a mini treasure hunt that keeps restarting all day.

Pro tip from me: play with friends. Split up, cover more ground, share locations. The Team Activity mode they added is perfect for this — it expands the Stork delivery co-op stuff from Part 2.

## Springtide Eggs and Season 4 Pass

Two new pet eggs dropped with Part 3 — the **Springtide Egg** and the **Premium Springtide Egg**. These are event-exclusive, so once Easter is over, they're GONE. Must-catch energy.

There's also a brand new **Season 4 Pass** with 50 levels of rewards. Fifty! That's a lot of free stuff if you actually grind it. Plus all the Part 2 goodies like the Candy Blossom plant and Evil Bunny rewards are still around.

## You Have Until April 26 — GO GO GO

Here's the bad news: the event ends **April 26, 2026**. That's like three days from now as I'm writing this. After that, the Easter Plot, the Choc Coins shop, the Springtide Eggs — all gone until who-knows-when.

So what are you still doing reading this? Go plant some Easter seeds, catch a Springtide Egg, and flex that pet bunny in chat. I'll see you in the garden. 🌱

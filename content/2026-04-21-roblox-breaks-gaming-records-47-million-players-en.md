# Roblox Just BROKE Gaming History — 47 Million Players at Once!

Okay besties, you are NOT going to believe this. Roblox just did something nobody thought was possible — it straight-up beat Steam, the biggest PC gaming platform EVER, for the all-time concurrent player record.

We're talking **47.3 million players online at the exact same time**. That's more people than live in the entire country of Spain. All playing Roblox. Together. My brain is melting.

## How Did This Even Happen?

It all went down during a wild weekend "war" between two viral Roblox games. Steam's old record was 41.2 million (set back in March 2025), and Roblox smashed right through it. For context — the last record holder was the whole Steam platform combined. Roblox did it with just a handful of games!

## Grow a Garden is INSANE

You already know Grow a Garden — that chill farming game where you plant seeds and watch them grow. Well, since launching in March 2025, it pulled in **21.19 BILLION visits**. Billion with a B.

It even scored a **Guinness World Record** for "Most concurrent players for a videogame" with 21.6 million players at once. Then on August 23, 2025, it pushed even higher to **22,346,725 players** at the same time. That's like every kid in California plus every kid in Texas logging in together!

The biggest updates that broke the internet? Blood Moon (5 million players), Bizzy Bees (11.7 million), and the Summer Update (21.3 million). Pure chaos. Pure vibes.

## Steal a Brainrot Stole the Crown

Just when we thought Grow a Garden was unbeatable, along came **Steal a Brainrot** in September 2025. This game blasted past 25 million concurrent players and snatched the Roblox CCU crown. The rivalry between these two is the main character energy of 2025-2026.

## The All-Time Visit Champions

Here are the GOATs of Roblox visits as of March 2026:

1. **Brookhaven RP** — 80.3 billion visits (absolute legend)
2. **Blox Fruits** — 52.95 billion visits
3. **Adopt Me!** — 40 billion visits
4. **Grow a Garden** — 21.19 billion visits (in ONE year!!)

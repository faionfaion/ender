# Obby Pro Mode: 7 Movement Tricks That Will Carry You Through Tower of Hell

Hey gamers, EnderFaion here! So I've been GRINDING obbies all week and oh my gosh, I have to share what I learned. If you keep falling into lava on the same jump over and over (we've ALL been there), this article is literally for you.

Obbies are one of the OG Roblox genres — they've been around since basically forever. And the best part? They're skill-pure. No pay-to-win, just you vs. the platforms. Let's get you carrying lobbies.

## The Obbies Everyone Is Playing in 2026

**Tower of Hell** is still the king, no contest. Rounds reset every 6 minutes, no checkpoints, randomly-generated tower — pure chaos in the best way.

If Tower of Hell makes you cry (it's okay, it makes me cry too), try **Mega Fun Obby** — it has over 2,400 levels with different color themes, so you'll never run out. **Escape Grandma's House** is the most beginner-friendly one with funny moments, and **The Really Easy Obby** has skip passes if you get stuck. No shame in skipping. None.

## The 7 Tricks That Actually Work

**1. Mini Jump.** Don't HOLD spacebar — just TAP it. You'll do a tiny precise hop instead of a giant launch. Lifesaver for those tiny floating blocks.

**2. Turn off Shift Lock.** I know everyone says to use it, but for most obbies the free camera is way easier. ONLY turn it on for angled jumps where you need to aim sideways.

**3. Edge Jumping.** Walk all the way to the edge of a block before you jump. You get extra distance basically for free. Sounds simple but it's HUGE.

**4. Air Control.** Tilt your camera mid-jump to nudge where you land. Most people don't know you can still steer in the air. Now you do.

**5. Bunny Hopping.** Mash spacebar while running, and time each jump for the exact moment you land. Keeps your momentum going so you fly across long sections.

**6. Wait for the SAVED prompt.** At checkpoints, do NOT move until you see the save text appear. I've lost so much progress because I was too excited. Don't be me.

**7. Observe before you jump.** Especially in Tower of Hell — spend two seconds watching the patterns. Lava-shooters, swinging blocks, disappearing platforms — they all have a rhythm.

Now go climb that tower. You got this!

## Bye-Bye Premium, Hello Roblox Plus!

Okay guys, this is HUGE. Roblox just dropped a massive announcement — **Roblox Plus** is launching on **April 30, 2026**, and it's replacing the old Roblox Premium subscription we all know. If you've been rocking Premium, don't panic — I've got all the details right here!

## What Is Roblox Plus?

Roblox Plus is the brand-new monthly subscription that costs **$4.99 USD per month**. It comes packed with perks that honestly sound pretty fire. You get a **10% discount** on avatar items and in-game purchases right away. But here's the clutch part — if you stay subscribed for **3 months in a row**, that discount jumps to **20%**. That's a serious save!

## Free Private Servers and Robux Transfers

One of my favorite perks? **Unlimited free private servers** across every game that supports them. No more paying extra just to vibe with your besties in peace. Plus, you can now **send Robux to other players for free** — zero transaction fees. Want to gift your friend some Robux for their birthday? Easy peasy.

You also get access to **item trading** and can **resell avatar items** on the Marketplace. If you're into collecting rare stuff, this is a total game-changer.

## But Wait — Where Are My Monthly Robux?

Here's the thing: the base Roblox Plus plan does **not** include a monthly Robux stipend. I know, I know — that's different from old Premium. BUT Roblox is rolling out optional bundles called **Plus 500, Plus 1000, and Plus 2000** that give you that many Robux every month on top of all the Plus perks. So you can pick what works for your budget!

## Already Have Premium?

If you're currently a Premium subscriber, you're in luck. You get to **keep your existing plan** AND score a **free 1-month trial** of Roblox Plus stacked right on top. Just heads up — starting **May 30**, the 10% bonus on extra Robux purchases for Premium members goes away.

## Creators Win Too

Game developers are eating good with this update. They can earn **250 Robux per month** for every new subscriber they bring in through a special API — up to **750 Robux total** over 3 months per player. That's a pretty sweet deal for your favorite creators!

Roblox Plus drops April 30 — mark your calendars and get ready for a whole new era of perks. GG Roblox, this one's looking like a W! 🎮

## What's New in Roblox Studio

Roblox has pushed out a pretty significant update to Studio this month, and creators are going to love it. Here's the rundown:

**New Terrain Sculpting Brush** — You can now sculpt terrain with way more precision. The new brush has adjustable softness, pressure sensitivity, and even a symmetry mode. If you've ever tried to make realistic mountains in Studio, you know how painful it used to be. Not anymore!

**Improved Lighting Editor** — The lighting panel got a complete overhaul. You can now preview different times of day in real-time without running the experience. Plus, there's a new volumetric fog system that makes horror games look absolutely *chef's kiss*.

**Script Auto-Complete 2.0** — The script editor now has smarter auto-complete that actually understands your project structure. It suggests functions from your own modules, not just the Roblox API. This is going to save SO much time.

## UGC Catalog Changes

This is the part that's got everyone talking. Roblox is rolling out a **new verification system** for UGC creators:

- **Creator Badges** — Verified creators now get a visible badge on their items in the catalog. This should help buyers know they're getting quality stuff.
- **Revenue Split Update** — Creators of limited items will now earn 40% instead of 30%. That's a massive bump!
- **New Moderation Pipeline** — Items will go through a faster review process, with most approvals happening within 24 hours instead of the usual 3-5 days.

## Community Reaction

The creator community is mostly hyped. The terrain tools and revenue bump are getting the most love. Some folks are worried that the new verification system might make it harder for new creators to get started, but Roblox says the requirements will be "accessible to anyone who consistently creates quality content."

I think this is a really solid update. The Studio improvements alone make it worth celebrating, and the UGC changes show that Roblox is actually listening to creators. W update, no cap.

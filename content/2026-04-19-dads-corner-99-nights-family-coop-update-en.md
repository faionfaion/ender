## Dad's Corner is Back (and Yes, I Brought Snacks)

Hey gang, FaionEnder here. Big news from the Roblox world, and for once it's news that actually makes my dad-heart happy. Roblox just made it way easier for families to play together, AND my favorite game — 99 Nights in the Forest — dropped a brand new update last week. Grab a juice box, let me explain.

## Roblox Kids and Roblox Select: What Just Happened

On April 13, 2026, Roblox rolled out two new account types: **Roblox Kids** for ages 5–8 and **Roblox Select** for ages 9–15. Basically, the games, chat, and settings now match your age automatically. Safer, simpler, and parents get real controls over screen time, friend lists, and spending.

Here's the part I love: Connected Parents are automatically a **Trusted Friend**. Translation — I can officially join my daughter's game without any awkward setup. She says this is "kinda cringe but also kinda cool." I'll take it.

## Trusted Friends: The Inner Circle

Rolled out April 2, Trusted Friends is your squad of family and close buddies. With it on, you get Party Voice and natural chat, so co-op actually feels like co-op. For kids under 9, chat stays off unless a parent flips the switch for trusted people only. Smart system, honestly.

Older siblings, cousins, and yes — embarrassing dads — can now jump into the same server without jumping through ten hoops.

## 99 Nights in the Forest: What Dropped on April 11

Now the good stuff. The devs of 99 Nights in the Forest launched their update on **April 11, 2026 at 13:00 EST**, and I've been living in that forest ever since. Here's what landed:

The big headline is the new co-op tuning that plays perfectly with Trusted Friends — server stability is smoother, and joining a family member's run is basically one tap. Night survival feels way less scary when Dad's carrying the spare torches.

Before that, March and early April gave us a stack of goodies that are still live: **Easter Part 2** arrived April 4 with two new Easter Eggs and a limited Bunny Class for 900 event currency in the Easter Shop. Earlier in March we got the Mini Fairy Biome with the giant Mother Tree to restore, a Blue Chest, the Botany Badge, a Fairy Wolf, and glowing firefly plants for nighttime runs. The Jungle Biome Part 2 added the Cat Entity and Mossy Mammoths. Hard Mode is also live, for the brave ones.

If you haven't logged in since the 11th — you've got homework, squad.

## Dad Wisdom of the Day

Restoring the Mother Tree takes patience. Surviving 99 nights takes even more. That's the secret of survival games — rushing gets you eaten, chilling together gets you to morning. Same goes for life, but let's not get weird about it.

So here's the plan: approve the game in parental settings, link up as Trusted Friends, hop in tonight, and catch up on everything the April 11 patch brought. I'll bring the torches. You bring the berries.

See you in the forest, squad.

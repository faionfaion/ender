# Roblox Studio Just Got an AI That Builds AND Plays Your Game!

Okay gamers, you are NOT going to believe what Roblox just dropped. On April 16, 2026, Roblox announced that Studio Assistant is going full agentic — which is a fancy way of saying the AI can now plan your game, build it, AND playtest it all by itself. Wild, right?

And get this: 44% of the top 1,000 Roblox creators are already using AI tools in Studio. So this isn't some tiny update — this is the future of making games on Roblox.

## Planning Mode Is the Real MVP

You know how starting a new game can feel super overwhelming? Planning Mode fixes that. You just tell Assistant what kind of game you want — like a tycoon or an obby — and it writes up a mini game design document for you.

It reads your existing code, asks you questions like a real teammate, and builds a step-by-step to-do list. Here's the best part: YOU get to review and edit the plan BEFORE anything changes. You're still the boss, the AI is just your super-fast helper.

## The Playtest Agent Is Kinda Insane

This one blew my mind. There's a brand-new beta feature called the Playtest Agent, and it literally plays your game for you.

It jumps into your world, uses a virtual mouse and keyboard, takes screenshots, reads the logs, and checks if everything works like your plan said it should. If it finds a bug? It tells Assistant, and Assistant fixes it. Plan, build, test, fix — all in one loop. It's like having a free QA tester friend that never sleeps.

## Type It, Get It in 3D

There's also Mesh Generation and Scene Generation. Type something like "mossy dungeon with torches" and boom — you get a rough 3D layout you can drop into your game. Need a prop? Describe it and it appears with textures already on it.

Plus, Luau Assist can now read ALL your scripts at once instead of just one file, so it actually understands how your whole game fits together.

## What Else Is Coming April 30

Same rollout window is packed. Studio now has a built-in MCP Server that connects to tools like Claude, Cursor, and Gemini. The Character Controller Library is fully released, Animation Graph is in beta, and Avatar Makeup just hit the Marketplace. Roblox Plus also launches April 30, 2026, with Robux now working as a subscription payment option.

Oh — and if you're testing a mobile game in Studio, you can now do pinch, rotate, and two-finger pan with just your mouse. Finally!

## My Take

If you've been too scared to try scripting, THIS is your moment. Planning Mode basically hands you a recipe, and the Playtest Agent catches your mistakes before your friends do. Go open Studio Beta and try it — I'm already planning my next obby with it. GG Roblox, you cooked.

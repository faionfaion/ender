# Roblox Plus Drops April 30! Plus PS5 Upgrade & New Kids Accounts

Okay gamers, GRAB A SNACK because April 2026 is hands down the biggest Roblox month I've seen in forever. We're getting a whole new subscription, a faster PS5 app, and even new account types for younger players. Let me break it all down!

## Bye-Bye Premium, Hello Roblox Plus

Big news first: Roblox Premium is officially retiring, and on April 30, 2026, the brand-new **Roblox Plus** takes over. It costs $4.99 a month and honestly, the perks slap.

You get 10% off all in-game and avatar items right away. Stay subscribed for 3 months in a row and that discount jumps to a juicy 20% — yes, more fits for less Robux.

But wait, there's more! Roblox Plus members get free unlimited private servers (huge for hanging with friends), exclusive avatar items, AND — this is the wild part — you can send Robux to other users with zero transaction fees. Gifting your bestie just got way easier.

If you already have Premium, chill — you can keep it. But no new Premium sign-ups from here on out.

## PS5 Players, You Eating Good

On April 14, Roblox launched a native PlayStation 5 app and it's a total glow-up. We're talking up to 30% faster load times compared to the old version.

It's built specifically for PS5 hardware, so games run smoother, menus feel snappier, and you'll stop rage-quitting because of loading screens. Console squad finally getting the respect they deserve.

## New Accounts Just for Younger Players

Starting early June 2026, Roblox is rolling out two new account types to keep younger players in safer zones:

- **Roblox Kids** (ages 5–8): curated catalog of thousands of games rated Minimal or Mild content only.
- **Roblox Select** (ages 9–15): access up to Moderate content after a quick three-step setup.

Age gets confirmed through age-check tech or parent verification, and parental controls get a serious upgrade too. Basically, the platform is getting safer without being boring.

## Quick Hits for the Dev-Curious

A few more drops worth knowing: voice chat now supports up to 100 users in one server, real-time profanity rephrasing keeps chat chill, and the Character Controller Library went full release — meaning smoother movement in brand-new games.

Oh, and Dandy's World has a massive in-game event running through April 24 with structured rewards. Go farm those prizes before it ends!

# Easter Part 2 in Grow a Garden Is Going OFF Right Now!

Hey garden gang, it's me, EnderFaion, and I literally just logged off Grow a Garden to write this because I HAD to tell you. Easter Part 2 just dropped and my farm is glowing pink with Candy Blossoms. This event is the biggest thing happening on Roblox this April, no cap.

## The Candy Blossom is the new main character

Okay so the star of the show is the Candy Blossom seed. To craft one, you need 20 Golden Eggs (or 10 Premium Golden Eggs if you're fancy) plus 1 Candy Blossom Shard. Those shards drop every 25 fruits you feed to the Angry Plant NPC, so keep farming and feeding!

Golden Eggs come from Tony NPC, and here's the catch — they start at 25,000 Choc Coins and the price DOUBLES every time you buy one. Yeah. So budget your coins like a little accountant, friends.

Oh, and shoutout to everyone who already owned the original Candy Blossom — you get free Elder Candy Blossom Upgrades equal to however many you had. Loyalty pays off!

## Evil Bunny quests and free seed packs

The Evil Bunny is back with chaos quests — he picks a plant, you have 30 minutes to destroy it, and the rarer the plant, the juicier the loot. It's honestly kinda funny watching your friends sacrifice their sparkly plants for prizes.

Every submission gets you 1 Easter Seed Pack, and every 50 submissions = 1 Exotic Easter Seed Pack. Free stuff? Yes please.

## Wait, there's more community hype

While we're farming, the Park Expo Collective just threw PEC26 on April 4-5 — a whole in-Roblox expo where fans show off theme parks they built themselves. New rides, new booths, creators hanging out live. Proof that you can literally build your own Roblox community event, not just play them.

Also, heads up: Roblox just renamed Connections back to Friends on April 7 (finally, way less confusing), and on April 30 the old Premium sub is getting replaced by Roblox Plus. Big changes month!

## Save the date

And for the long-game planners — RDC26, the huge Roblox Developers Conference, is happening September 10-12 in San Jose. That's where Roblox announces what's coming next to the platform. Mark your calendars!

For now though? Get back in Grow a Garden and grind those Golden Eggs before Tony's prices get ridiculous. See you on the farm! 🌸

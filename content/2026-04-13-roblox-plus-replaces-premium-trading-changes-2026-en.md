OMG guys, HUGE news just dropped and every Roblox trader needs to hear this!

## Say Hello to Roblox Plus

Roblox has announced a brand-new subscription called **Roblox Plus** — and it's set to replace Roblox Premium for all new subscribers. That means if you don't have Premium yet, Roblox Plus will be your go-to plan going forward.

So what do you get? Trading access, discounts on avatar items, free private servers, and the ability to transfer Robux. Basically all the good stuff traders love, bundled into one plan!

## What If You Already Have Premium?

Don't worry! If you're already a Premium member, Roblox says you keep ALL your current benefits. Nothing changes for you. Existing Premium members are also reportedly getting a **free trial** of Roblox Plus on top of their current sub. Nice!

## What This Means for Trading

Whether you're on Roblox Plus or legacy Premium, you'll still trade **Limited** and **Limited U** items — look for that green banner on the item icon. With UGC Limiteds getting bigger every month, there are way more items to trade than ever before!

## Stay Safe, Friends!

Quick reminder — scammers love targeting traders. Here are the basics:

- **Never click suspicious links** people send you, even if they say it's their "portfolio" or a "private server." Fake links can steal your login info and **take over your account** — even if you have **two-step verification** turned on (that's the extra security code Roblox sends to your email or phone when you log in).
- **Double-check every trade** before you accept. Trades are final — no take-backs!
- **Never do "trust trades"** or trade outside the official Roblox trading window. If someone says "you go first," that's a big red flag.
- **Turn on two-step verification** if you haven't already! Ask a parent or guardian to help you set it up — it takes just a couple of minutes.

And if you ever want to look up item values on other websites, **always ask a parent or guardian first** before visiting any site outside of Roblox.

Don't rush your trades! Take your time, compare what you're giving with what you're getting, and never let anyone pressure you into a quick deal. Patient traders are happy traders! 💚

# Grow a Garden Easter Part 2 Just Dropped — And It's Huge!

Okay, stop what you're doing. Seriously. Grow a Garden just dropped Easter Event Part 2 on April 12, and it is the biggest update this game has had all year. If you haven't logged in yet, you are missing out on SO much loot.

For real though — this game went from zero to over 21 BILLION visits in 2026. It's the fastest-growing new Roblox game in history, hitting 250K concurrent players at peak. That's basically everyone you know planting carrots at the same time.

## 18 New Crops and 6 Wild Mutations

Part 2 dropped 18 brand new seeds — 9 Easter-themed and 9 from Season 4. The star of the show? The new Candy Blossom crop that looks straight out of a cartoon.

And the mutations are next level. We're talking Choc, Plasma, Gummy, Ash, Haze, and Smoldering. Basically your boring old tomato can now be a glowing plasma tomato. The flex is unreal.

## 10 New Pets To Collect

This is where I lost my mind. Ten new pets joined the roster, including Firemite, Ash Raven, Hazehound, Cerberus, Chocolate Bunny, Marshmallow Lamb, Gummy Bear, and Hootsie Roll.

Grow a Garden now has 121 total pets across 7 rarity tiers — Common all the way up to Prismatic. Pets roam your garden, take little naps, and boost your crop growth or mutation chances. Stack the same pet and their abilities stack too. Pro tip right there.

Oh, and the new Golden Egg can hatch into 4 different Easter pets. Fingers crossed for the Chocolate Bunny.

## Easter Garden Is a Whole Separate Plot

Here's the coolest part — you can switch to an entirely new plot called Easter 2026 in the plot selector. It only exists during the event.

You plant Easter crops, sell them at Easter stands for Chocolate Coins, then spend those coins at the special Easter Seed Shop. Plus, every 15 minutes there's an Egg Hunt where you search the map for 10 hidden eggs for random rewards. Free loot every 15 minutes?? Yes please.

## Season 4 Pass Is Stacked

There's also a new 50-level Season 4 Pass packed with seeds, pets, and a new currency. And if you grind hard enough, there are Evil Bunny rewards waiting at the end.

No cap, this is the best time ever to jump into Grow a Garden. Cozy vibes, zero stress, tons of collectibles. Go plant something weird and send me a screenshot!

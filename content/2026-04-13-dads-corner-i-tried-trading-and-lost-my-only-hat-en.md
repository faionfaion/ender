## The Day Dad Became Hatless

Okay, kids. Sit down. Grab a snack. Your old pal FaionEnder needs to confess something embarrassing.

I lost my favorite hat in a Roblox trade. My ONE good hat. The hat I wore in every single game, including all 99 nights in the forest. Gone. Poof. Traded away like yesterday's lunch.

## How It All Started

So there I was, minding my own business, when some player messaged me about a trade. They had this super shiny accessory — looked incredible, had a RAP (that's Recent Average Price) of like 15,000 Robux. My hat was worth maybe 2,000. I thought I was getting the deal of a LIFETIME.

"Dad's gonna be a trading genius," I told EnderFaion. She just sighed. She sighs a lot when I'm about to do something questionable.

## The Rookie Mistake

Here's what I did NOT do: check Rolimons (ask your parents before visiting any outside site!). For those who don't know, Rolimons is a website where you can look up the REAL value of any Limited item. Experienced traders use it before every single deal. I, a very confident and very foolish dad, did not.

Turns out that shiny accessory was what traders call a "projected" item — someone artificially pumped up its price by trading it to themselves at crazy high amounts. The real value? Basically nothing.

I traded my beautiful hat for a digital paperweight.

## What I Learned (The Hard Way)

Here's what every trader needs to know:

**You need Premium to trade.** Only Roblox Premium members can use the trading system, and only Limited or Limited U items can be swapped.

**Roblox takes a 30% fee on any Robux in a trade.** Always factor that in.

**Check the trade window TWICE before hitting Accept.** Scammers love the bait-and-switch — they show you a great item, then swap it for junk right before you confirm.

**Trades are permanent.** Roblox will not undo a completed trade. Once you click Accept, it's done forever. No take-backs. No dad-privilege overrides.

**Never trade outside of Roblox.** If someone asks you to use Discord, a random website, or a "middleman" — run. That's a scam, every single time.

## Dad's Final Wisdom

EnderFaion told me I should have started small and practiced before going all-in on my precious hat. She was right. She's always right. Don't tell her I said that.

If you're thinking about jumping into trading — please, PLEASE learn from my disaster. Check values first. Read the trade window. And protect your hats.

They're more important than you think.

*— FaionEnder, proud owner of zero hats*

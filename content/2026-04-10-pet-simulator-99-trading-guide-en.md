## Scam Protection Tips

Trading in PS99 can be risky. Here's how to stay safe:

1. **Always check values BEFORE trading** — bookmark this guide or use a value site like PS99 Values
2. **Never trust "trust trades"** — if someone asks you to give your pet first, it's a scam. 100% of the time
3. **Watch out for "overpay" tricks** — someone offers 10 bad pets for your 1 good pet? The 10 bad ones are probably worth less combined
4. **Screenshot everything** — if something goes wrong, you have proof for reporting
5. **Use the official trading plaza** — random trade requests in other games are sketchy
6. **Don't fall for fake giveaways** — "Drop your best pet and I'll dupe it" is the oldest scam in the book
7. **Check pet enchantments** — make sure the pet in the trade window matches what was promised (some scammers swap pets last second)

## Quick Trading Tips

- **Buy low during events, sell high after** — event pets are cheap while the event runs, then spike in value
- **Rainbow pets are ALWAYS worth more** — if you can rainbow a pet, do it before trading
- **Bundle trades are powerful** — 3-4 A-tier pets can get you an S-tier if you find the right person
- **Patience pays off** — don't panic-sell when a new update drops and values dip temporarily
- **Watch content creators** — big YouTubers can crash or spike pet values overnight

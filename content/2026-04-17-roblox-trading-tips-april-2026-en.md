# Roblox Trading in 2026: How to Trade Smart and Spot Scams

Hey besties, EnderFaion here! Trading is BUZZING right now after the surprise March 2026 Sale, and with Roblox Plus dropping on April 30, things are about to get even wilder. So let me break down how to trade like a pro without getting scammed.

## How Trading Actually Works

First things first: to trade on Roblox you need an active Roblox Premium subscription and a verified account. No Premium, no trades — that's the rule.

You can only trade Limited and Limited U items. Regular stuff and most UGC items? Nope, those stay locked in your inventory forever. And every trade HAS to happen inside the official Roblox trade window — once you hit confirm, there's no undo button.

## The Two Numbers You Need to Know

Before you trade anything, check two values. **RAP (Recent Average Price)** is Roblox's own number showing recent sales — but small trader groups can pump it up to trick people.

That's why smart traders also check **Community Value** on sites like Rolimon's and Traderie. These track what items ACTUALLY sell for. For Adopt Me! players, Traderie and AdoptMeValues.app are your besties. Top pets in April 2026? Shadow Dragon, Frost Dragon, Bat Dragon, Giraffe, Blue Dog, and Pink Cat are still ruling the charts.

## What's Hot Right Now

The March 2026 Sale ran March 20 to April 2, bringing back eight retired limiteds — one per day! Fresh drops included the Pinstripe Fedora, Lampshade, Bunny Ears, and Oozing Oscar. And on April 1, a Roblox admin showed up in Trade Hangout hinting at a secret bonus item. Spicy!

Roblox Plus launches April 30 for $4.99/month with marketplace discounts and private server perks. Could shake up item values big time.

## Scams to Block Every Single Time

**Trust trades** — if someone says "send your item first, I'll send mine after," RUN. That's always a scam.

**Beam links** — fake GFX portfolios or "free private server" links can steal your cookies and skip right past 2-Step Verification. Never click sus links.

**Middlemen** — anyone offering to "hold" your items outside the trade window is lying. The official trade window is the ONLY safe place.

**Last-second switches** — scammers swap a rare item for trash right before you confirm. Always re-check every single item before you click that button.

## Your Safety Checklist

1. Turn on 2-Step Verification in Settings → Security.
2. Never share your password — not with "friends," not with fake "support."
3. Check values on Rolimon's or Traderie first.
4. Double-check every item in the trade window.
5. Report scammers with the in-game Report Abuse button.

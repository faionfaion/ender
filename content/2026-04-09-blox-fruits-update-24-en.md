## Update 24 just changed everything

Hey hey, EnderFaion here! 🎮

Blox Fruits players, the wait is OVER. **Update 24** dropped on April 6 and it's absolutely stacked. New fruits, a brand new map, two raid bosses, and a bunch of quality-of-life changes. I've been grinding non-stop since it came out, so let me break it all down for you.

## New fruits

### Abyss Fruit (Mythical)

This is the star of the update. The **Abyss Fruit** is a new Mythical-tier fruit with a deep sea theme. Here's what makes it OP:

- **Z move — Depth Crush:** Creates a pressure zone that slows and damages enemies. Insane crowd control.
- **X move — Abyssal Tentacles:** Summons dark tentacles that grab and throw enemies. Works amazing in PvP.
- **C move — Void Dive:** You literally phase into the ground and become untargetable for 3 seconds. Then pop up with an AOE explosion.
- **V move — Leviathan's Rage:** The ultimate. Summons a massive sea creature that deals mega damage in a huge area.

Verdict? It's top 5 fruits in the game right now. Maybe top 3 if you're good at combos.

### Coral Fruit (Legendary)

A solid Legendary addition with a defensive focus:

- Creates coral barriers that block projectiles
- Has a healing move that restores 15% HP over time
- The awakened version (coming later) is rumored to have a full coral armor transformation

Not as flashy as Abyss, but super useful for team play and raids.

## New map: The Sunken Kingdom

This is a **Third Sea** underwater zone and it's absolutely beautiful. The devs went hard on this one.

- **Size:** About the same as Floating Turtle Island
- **Theme:** Ancient underwater ruins with bioluminescent plants and coral structures
- **NPCs:** Level 2800-2950 enemies (so yeah, endgame content)
- **Quests:** 8 new quests with solid XP rewards
- **Secret area:** There's a hidden cave behind the main temple — I won't spoil what's inside, but FIND IT

The underwater movement mechanics feel smooth. You can swim freely in this zone without needing a specific fruit, which is a nice touch.

## New raid bosses

### The Kraken Lord (Raid Boss #1)

Located in the center of The Sunken Kingdom. This is a **Level 3000 raid boss** and it does NOT mess around.

**Key mechanics:**
- Summons tentacle waves you need to dodge
- Has a whirlpool phase where the whole arena spins
- Enrages at 25% HP and gains a one-shot attack (watch for the red glow!)
- Drops the **Kraken Trident** accessory (2% drop rate)

Recommended: Bring at least 4 players. Solo is technically possible but you'll need top-tier fruits and gear.

### Poseidon (Raid Boss #2)

Found after completing a quest chain in the Sunken Kingdom. This is the **true final boss** of the update.

**Key mechanics:**
- Three phases with different attack patterns
- Spawns water clones of himself in phase 2
- Phase 3 floods the arena and you fight while swimming
- Drops **Poseidon's Crown** (1% drop rate) — gives +15% damage in water zones

This fight is genuinely hard. I failed it like 6 times before we figured out the clone phase. GG to the devs for making it challenging.

## Quality-of-life changes

The devs also snuck in some much-needed fixes:

- **Fruit storage expanded** from 2 to 3 slots
- **Stat reset** is now free once per week (used to cost Robux!)
- **Trading UI** got a complete overhaul — way less clunky
- **Server hopping** is faster with a new "Quick Join" button
- **Bug fixes** for Buddha fruit hitbox issues and Leopard transformation glitches

## My verdict

Update 24 is a **9/10** for me. The new map is gorgeous, the Abyss Fruit is a blast to play, and the raid bosses are genuinely challenging. The only thing missing is more content for mid-game players — this update is heavily focused on endgame.

If you haven't played Blox Fruits in a while, now is the time to come back. See you in The Sunken Kingdom! ⚔️

# Grow a Garden's Easter Part 2 Just Dropped — And It's Wild

Okay friends, we NEED to talk about Grow a Garden because this game is straight-up breaking Roblox right now. It just hit over 10 million concurrent players — that's the biggest peak ANY Roblox game has ever had. Ever. And it's already racked up more than 21 billion total visits in record time.

If you haven't tried it yet, the vibe is super chill AFK farming: buy seeds, plant them, harvest, sell, repeat. Simple, cozy, kinda addictive.

## What's New in Easter Part 2

On April 12, 2026, the devs dropped the second half of the Easter event and it's packed. We're talking new pets, new plants, fresh gear, and a pile of cosmetics you can grind for. The whole garden basically got a pastel glow-up.

The star of the update is Candy Blossom, a brand-new plant that looks exactly as sweet as it sounds. It fits right in with the Chocolate Coin currency that came with Part 1, so you can stack those coins by growing seasonal crops.

## Meet the Evil Bunny

Okay this part is hilarious. There's a new NPC called the Evil Bunny, and no, he's not actually scary — he's more mischievous than menacing. He'll give you a task: grab a shovel and destroy a specific plant in your garden. Do it, and you get rewards. Kinda brutal for a farming game, but the community is eating it up.

It's giving "chaotic garden goblin energy" and I'm here for it.

## Season Pass 4 Is Still Going

Don't forget Season Pass 4 is still active from the April 4 update. It's got 50 reward levels, an Easter Seed Shop, and an exclusive Easter Garden plot you can't get any other way. If you've been grinding, you're probably halfway through by now.

Pro tip: log in daily and just leave the game running while you do homework. AFK farming basically plays itself.

## A "New Era" for Grow a Garden

The developers just announced the game is entering a "new era" focused on deeper farming mechanics. Translation: more strategy, more stuff to do, fewer boring patches. After some updates fans weren't thrilled with, this feels like a real glow-up for the game.

So yeah — if you haven't jumped back in, now's the moment. Grab those chocolate coins, plant a Candy Blossom, and maybe don't trust the bunny. See you in the garden!

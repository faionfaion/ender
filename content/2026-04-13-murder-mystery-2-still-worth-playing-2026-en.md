## Over a Decade and Still Going Strong

Okay gamers, can we talk about how Murder Mystery 2 just REFUSES to slow down? Nikilis launched this game back in January 2014 — that's over twelve years ago — and it's sitting at a mind-blowing **24.9 billion visits**. Yeah, billion with a B. It pulls in **150,000+ players** at pretty much any time of day, and honestly? It's earned every single one of those numbers.

## What Makes MM2 So Addictive

If you somehow haven't tried it yet (where have you been?!), here's the deal. Every round you get one of three roles: **Innocent**, **Sheriff**, or **Murderer**. Innocents gotta survive and figure out who's the bad guy. The Sheriff is the only one with a weapon to stop the Murderer. And the Murderer? Well, they're trying to get everyone. Rounds are super quick so you're never bored, and there are three modes to pick from — **Casual**, **Hardcore**, and **Assassin**.

## Valentine's Event Was Fire

The biggest update of 2026 so far was the **Valentine's Event** running from February 13 to March 16. The lobby got a total makeover with Valentine's vibes, plus we got **Daily Quests** and a brand-new Mystery Box. You could earn **Heart Gems** — up to 30 per day by collecting 240 coins — and use them to open the Valentine's Box '26 for exclusive gear skins. Some of those cosmetics looked absolutely sick.

## The Trading Scene Is Wild

Let's be real — half the fun of MM2 is the trading. The value game is no joke right now. **Chroma Evergun** is sitting at over **100,000 value**, making it the most sought-after item in the game. **Chroma Evergreen** comes in at 69,000 and **Chroma Alienbeam** at 33,000. Even regular Godlys like the **Traveler's Gun** are worth around 4,100. Values shift fast though, so always double-check before making a trade! If you're into the value game, ask a parent to help you find trusted community value lists to keep up with prices.

## Any Codes Right Now?

Bad news on this one, friends. As of April 2026, there are **zero active codes**. All the old ones have expired. But keep your eyes peeled because Nikilis usually drops new ones around big events, and people are already buzzing about a possible **Easter event** coming soon.

## The Verdict

Murder Mystery 2 is proof that a great concept never gets old. Sure, some rounds can feel a bit samey, but the trading keeps things fresh and those seasonal events always bring everyone back. With **19.6 million favorites** and **8 million likes**, the community has spoken. If you haven't jumped in lately, now's a great time to see what's new. Trust me, this classic still hits different!

## Dad Was Right All Along

Hey kids, FaionEnder here — and I'm about to do a victory lap around the living room. Remember when I kept saying '99 Nights in the Forest' was a big deal? Well, Hollywood finally agrees with me.

**20th Century Studios** (yes, the Disney-owned one) just won the theatrical rights to a movie based on our favorite spooky survival game. The deal was announced in April 2026, and the studio beat out a bunch of competitors to grab it.

## Who's Making It?

The game's creators — **Alec Kieft, Matthew Hufton, and Cameron Angland** from Grandma's Favourite Games — are on board as executive producers. **J.R. Young** is overseeing things for the studio.

No writer or director yet, but Disney live-action president **David Greenbaum** said the game 'has already connected with millions of players worldwide.' Dad translation: they saw the numbers and their jaws hit the floor.

## About Those Numbers

On April 18, the game hit **302,142 concurrent players** — that makes it the #4 game on all of Roblox right now. Lifetime visits? **26 billion**. Its all-time peak of **14.2 million players** makes it the #3 experience on the whole platform. Player rating: **90.6%**. Not bad for a game that launched March 4, 2025.

## Update Cadence Is Wild

The devs have been shipping stuff nearly every week. In the last month alone: the **Jungle Biome Part 2** (Cat Entity, Mossy Mammoths), the **Fairy Mini Biome**, two **Easter updates** with egg hunts and the limited-time **Bunny Class**, and a surprise 'mystery update' on April 11.

Also new lately: **Modifier Flames** (Coinsplosion is my favorite), the **Flowers system** with pot buffs, a **Tool Trader NPC**, and four new classes — Gunslinger, Grenadier, Nightcrawler, and Feaster.

## Dad's Wisdom Corner

Listen, I know you kids want to rush to Day 5 and swing big weapons. But real survival is patience. Here's what works for your old man:

- **Night 1:** The Deer doesn't attack. Grab that Bunny Foot and trade up through Wolf Pelt → Alpha Wolf Pelt → Bear Pelt.
- **Always cook your meat.** Raw meat = health penalty. This is also true in real life, by the way.
- **Farm carrots.** Infinite food → Crock Pot stew → half a hunger bar back. Patience pays.
- **The Deer hates light.** Flashlight in the face, then sprint home.

And honestly? 99 nights in a forest sounds a lot like my camping trips before GPS existed. See you by the campfire, kids.

# Roblox Easter 2026: Top Picks from 25+ Egg Hunts — Still Live & What You Missed!

Okay besties, grab your bunny ears because Roblox went WILD for Easter 2026! There's no big platform-wide event this year, but honestly? That's even better — because over 25 games ran their OWN egg hunts, and a few of them are STILL going right now.

I've been hopping between servers all month and my inventory is stacked. Let me spill the tea: which hunts you can still farm this week, and which legendary drops you sadly missed.

## Still Live Right Now — GO GO GO! 🐰

These are the hunts you can actually log into today (April 21), so prioritize these before they close:

- **Dandy's World** — until **April 24** (only a few days left! It has a cute advent-calendar system, so daily logins = more rewards)
- **Epic Minigames** — until **April 27** (the fastest egg farm on Roblox right now, no cap)
- **Horse Life** — until **May 2** (the longest window and the chillest hunt — perfect if you only have time for one)

## What You Missed (Rip 😭)

Okay, storytime for the besties who were offline. Two of the biggest Easter events already wrapped up, but here's what went down so you're not totally out of the loop.

### Brookhaven's 70-Egg Hunt (Ended April 3)

Brookhaven 🏡 RP ran its Easter Event from March 27 to April 3, 2026, with 70 eggs split across Easy, Medium, Hard, and Extreme difficulty. Yes, SEVENTY. Players grinded Bunny Tokens — 580 from Hard mode and a whopping 1,475 from Extreme — and traded them for the Spring Shuffler, the Easter Truck, the Easter House, and a bunch of cute spring props. If you grabbed the Extreme rewards, your Brookhaven house is straight-up iconic right now.

### Adopt Me! Sugarfest & the Legendary Easter Bunny (Ended April 13)

This one was massive. Adopt Me!'s Sugar Festival (aka Sugarfest: Game of Sweets) ran March 13 to April 13, 2026, and the star of the show was the Easter Bunny legendary pet that dropped April 4 through Admin Abuse (that's the in-game name for when Adopt Me! admins hand out super rare pets in live servers — not a real bug!). If a friend snagged one, you can still try trading for it, but it's gonna cost you.

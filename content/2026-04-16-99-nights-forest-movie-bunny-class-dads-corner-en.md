# Dad's Corner: They're Making a MOVIE of 99 Nights in the Forest!

Okay kids, gather round, because your old dad has some HUGE news. You know my favorite game — the one Faion always teases me about playing at 2 AM? Well, 99 Nights in the Forest is getting its very own Hollywood movie. I nearly spilled my coffee on the keyboard.

## Disney Is on the Case

20th Century Studios (yep, the Disney folks) just grabbed the movie rights after a big bidding war. The three game creators from Grandma's Favourite Games — Alec Kieft, Matthew Hufton, and Cameron Angland — are signed on as executive producers. That's a dad-pride moment if I've ever seen one.

Disney's David Greenbaum said the team has "world building ideas that feel innately cinematic." Fancy words, but I think it just means: spooky forest go brrrr. No writer or director yet, so we've got some waiting to do. (Patience, young padawan. More on that in a sec.)

## A Quick Flex on the Numbers

This game only launched on March 4, 2025 — barely a year old — and it's already the **7th most-played Roblox game of ALL TIME**. We're talking nearly **26 billion lifetime visits** and an all-time peak of **14.2 million** players at once. As of this week, 228,700 of us are still out here building camps and rescuing those four missing kids. Player rating: 90.6% positive. Not bad, huh?

## Easter Part 2: Meet the Bunny Class

As if the movie news wasn't enough, the Easter Part 2 update dropped on April 4 and brought the brand-new **Bunny Class** (900 Easter event currency). At Level 1 you get night vision, a higher jump, and speed boosts when you munch carrots. Careful though — eating meat damages you. Bunnies are bunnies.

Want Level 2? You have to eat **200 carrots**. Level 3? **400 carrots**. Four. Hundred. Carrots. See kids — this is what I mean about patience. You can't rush a good bunny. Plus you spawn with 40 Carrot Darts, which are basically veggie kunai. My kind of weapon.

There's also two new hidden Easter Eggs (Firefly Egg and Easter Bunny's Egg), new pet skins, furniture, gems, and limited-time crafting skins in the Easter Shop. A mystery update was teased for April 11 at 13:00 EST too.

## Dad's Takeaway

Look, this game eats up 99 nights — and now it's eating up Hollywood too. Sorry, had to. Faion says my jokes are "a crime against comedy," but she still laughs, so who's really winning?

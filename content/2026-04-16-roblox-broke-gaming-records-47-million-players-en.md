# Roblox Smashed Gaming History: 47 Million Players at ONCE!

Okay bestie, you need to sit down for this one. Roblox didn't just break a record — it shattered the entire gaming industry's biggest record ever. Like, forever-level history stuff!

## The 47 Million Player Moment

On August 23, 2025, a wild **47,296,412 players** were online in Roblox at the exact same time. That beat Steam's old record of 41,239,880 players from March 2025. Yep, Roblox is now officially the most-used gaming platform in history!

And wanna know why it popped off? Two game creators — SpyderSammy (Steal a Brainrot) and Jandel (Grow a Garden) — started a silly **admin war** between their games. Millions of us jumped in to watch the chaos. Iconic.

## Grow a Garden: The Cozy Speedrun King

Grow a Garden dropped in March 2025 and instantly became THE vibe. It smashed 1 billion visits in just **33 days** — fastest Roblox game EVER. Currently sitting at over 32 billion visits.

On that record-breaking August day, it hit 22,346,725 concurrent players and grabbed a **Guinness World Record** for most concurrent players in a Roblox game. Not bad for a chill farming sim, right?

## Steal a Brainrot Takes the Crown

Then Steal a Brainrot said "hold my sprinkler." In September 2025, it peaked at **24,136,040 concurrent players** — stealing the crown from Grow a Garden. The game now has 42 billion visits and even hit 60 billion total plays back in February 2026.

But the wildest part? In February 2026, **Bruno Mars performed a live concert inside Steal a Brainrot** — and 12,862,161 of us watched it live. That's another Guinness World Record: largest video game concert by a single artist. EVER.

## You Can Break Records Too!

Here's the coolest part. Back in November 2025, Guinness World Records teamed up with Roblox so **actual kids can set real world records**. The 1% Win Obby speedrun and the Ultimate Easy Obby Championship let players earn official GWR gold medals just by playing obbies. First time kids could hold a real Guinness record from their bedroom, no cap!

## The All-Time GOAT

Oh, and before we wrap — Brookhaven RP is STILL the most-played Roblox game of all time with **69.13 billion visits**. It's been ruling since April 2020 and shows no signs of slowing down.

So yeah, Roblox basically owns gaming history now. Go flex this at school tomorrow — you're literally playing on the biggest gaming platform humanity has ever made. Catch you in the next one!

# Roblox Just Dropped on PS5 — And It's WAY Faster!

Okay friends, DROP EVERYTHING because this is huge. On April 14, 2026, Roblox officially launched its brand-new native PS5 app, and let me tell you — it is NOT the same as before.

We're talking up to **30% faster load times**, smoother controls, and way better graphics. If you've ever sat there watching a Blox Fruits island load forever, you already know why this is massive news.

## Why The PS5 Version Is A Game-Changer

The old Roblox on console used to feel a little slow — not gonna lie. But this new native app was built specifically for PS5 hardware, so everything just… snaps.

Jumping between games, loading into Grow a Garden, running around Brookhaven — it all feels buttery smooth now. Console squad, we eatin' good.

## What You Can Play Right Now

All your favorites are there. The current top three on Roblox are absolute bangers:

- **Adopt Me!** — cozy pet-trading royalty, sitting at 531K players right now.
- **99 Nights in the Forest** — the spooky survival hit with 442K players. Each night gets harder. My dad FaionEnder is OBSESSED with this one, lol.

Dress to Impress, Doors, Fisch, Blade Ball, Blox Fruits — all there, all popping.

While you're hyped about PS5, there's even MORE coming. Roblox announced two new account types launching in early June: **Roblox Kids** for ages 5–8 and **Roblox Select** for ages 9–15. Both come with safer content filters and better parental controls, which honestly is a W for everyone.

And on April 30, Roblox Plus — a brand-new premium subscription — is dropping. No details yet on what's inside, but you KNOW I'll be covering it the second it goes live.

# Roblox Studio Just Got SUPER Smart — Build Games by Just Talking!

Okay friends, stop what you're doing because THIS is huge. On April 15, Roblox announced that Roblox Studio is officially going "agentic" — which is a fancy word that basically means the Studio Assistant can now DO stuff for you, not just give tips. We're talking actual game-building magic!

If you've ever opened Studio, stared at a blank baseplate, and felt your brain melt — this update is literally made for you.

## Plan, Build, Test — Now With AI Helpers

The new Assistant works in three modes and each one is cooler than the last.

**Planning Mode** is like having a super patient friend. It asks you questions like "What kind of obby do you want?" and then writes a step-by-step action plan. You get to review it and change stuff BEFORE Studio touches your game. And soon the plans will save between sessions, so you can log off, eat a snack, and pick up exactly where you left off.

**Mesh Generation** is wild. You type something like "wooden treasure chest" and BOOM — a textured 3D object appears. No more hunting the toolbox for an hour.

**Procedural Models** auto-generate buildings, shelves, chairs, and props from text prompts. Your dream café just built itself, basically.

## The Playtesting Agent Is the Real MVP

This one is my favorite. There's now an automated tester character that actually plays your game, checks if it matches your original plan, reads the error logs, and tells you what's broken. It's literally a robot QA tester doing your homework. Bug hunting? Handled.

## Kids Are Already Winning at This

Get this — 44% of the top 1,000 Roblox creators already use AI tools to build their games. Studio even connects to outside helpers like Claude, Cursor, and Codex through a new built-in MCP server. Translation: the pros are using this stuff, and now YOU have the same tools.

Plus the new Flexible Studio UI (rolled out in January) makes everything way cleaner on small laptop screens, and the Jumpstart program from March is specifically helping brand-new creators publish their very first game.

## Your Move, Builders

You don't need to be a pro coder anymore. You can literally describe the game you're imagining and Studio helps build it with you. So go make that pizza tycoon, that horror obby, that pet simulator you've been daydreaming about.

The only thing stopping you now is how weird your ideas can get — and honestly, weirder is better. Go cook!

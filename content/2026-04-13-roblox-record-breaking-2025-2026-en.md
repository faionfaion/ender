## Roblox Is Literally Rewriting the Record Books

Okay besties, I need you to sit down for this one because the numbers I'm about to drop are absolutely WILD. Roblox has been smashing world records left and right over the past year, and some of these stats are so big they don't even feel real. Let's get into it!

## Grow a Garden Changed Everything

Remember Grow a Garden? That chill little idle gardening game that dropped on March 26, 2025? Well, it became the fastest Roblox game EVER to hit 1 billion visits — and it did it in just 33 days. Thirty-three. Days.

But here's where it gets truly unhinged. On August 23, 2025, Grow a Garden hit 22.3 million players all playing at the same time. That earned it an official Guinness World Record for the most concurrently played video game ever, crushing Fortnite's old record of 15.3 million. A gardening game beat Fortnite. Let that sink in.

By October 2025, the game had racked up over 32 billion visits total. That's more than four times the entire world population!

## Brookhaven Hits 80 BILLION Visits

If you thought 32 billion was a lot, Brookhaven RP said "hold my Bloxy Cola." On March 16, 2026, it became the very first Roblox experience to reach 80 billion total visits. That's roughly 9.8 times every single person on Earth. Brookhaven has been the king of roleplay for ages, and it just keeps growing with cool updates like the Cherry Blossom Update and the Trucks and Trailers update.

## The Biggest Concert in Gaming History

Here's one that blew my mind. In February 2026, Steal a Brainrot hosted a virtual concert that pulled in over 12.8 million viewers watching at the same time. That's more people than the entire population of Sweden! It officially earned a Guinness World Record for the largest music concert in a videogame by a single artist. Gaming concerts are literally bigger than real-life stadiums now.

## Roblox by the Numbers in 2026

The platform itself is on another level right now. We're talking 381.8 million monthly active users, 144 million people logging in every single day, and a peak of 47.29 million concurrent players back in August 2025 — the highest EVER on any gaming platform.

Oh, and Blox Fruits just casually hit 60 billion visits in March 2026 too. No big deal, right?

## What This Means for Us

Roblox isn't just a game anymore — it's literally the biggest interactive platform on the planet. With over 4.4 million active experiences (another Guinness World Record, by the way), there's never been a better time to be part of this community. Whether you're growing gardens, exploring Brookhaven, or vibing at virtual concerts, we're all part of something record-breaking.

# Roblox Studio Just Went Agentic — Build a Game With ONE Prompt!

Okay besties, sit down because this might be the wildest Roblox news of the whole year. On April 16, Roblox dropped an announcement called "Roblox Studio is Going Agentic," and basically? You can now type what game you want and Studio will start building it for you. Yes, really.

I've been testing it all weekend and my brain is melting in the best way. Let me break it down.

## Planning Mode is the main character

The headline feature is the upgraded Roblox Assistant with something called **Planning Mode**. Instead of just spitting out code, it acts like a little co-builder buddy. It looks at your game, asks you smart questions ("Do you want the coins to respawn? How fast should the player run?"), and then makes an **editable action plan** before it touches anything.

That's huge because you stay in control. You can delete steps, add steps, or change the whole plan — THEN it builds. It even playtests for you: takes screenshots, presses keys, reads the output log, finds bugs, and fixes them. Like having a homework helper who actually does the homework right.

## Mesh Generation = instant 3D stuff

This is the part that made me scream. You type "a glowing purple mushroom lamp" or drag in a picture, and a few seconds later a fully textured 3D mesh drops into your world. No Blender, no YouTube tutorials, no crying. Just vibes.

And **Procedural Models** are just as cool. You get an AI-built object — say a bookcase — with sliders on it. Slide one way: 3 shelves. Slide the other: 12 shelves. Want more chairs around your table? Drag the slider. Zero scripting.

## Almost half of the top creators are already doing this

Roblox said **44% of the top 1,000 creators** already use Roblox Assistant or third-party AI tools (through something called MCP) to plan and test their games. Studio even has a built-in MCP Server now, so pro tools like Claude Code and Cursor can plug right in.

So no, this isn't some weird future thing. The biggest devs on the platform are ALREADY using it. We're just catching up.

## What's next?

Roblox said they're working on letting **multiple AI agents work at the same time** in the cloud — one coding, one testing, one building characters. Basically a whole tiny dev team inside your computer.

## EnderFaion's honest take

Real talk: you still need ideas, taste, and a good eye. The AI can build a bookcase, but it can't decide your game is about a haunted library on the moon. That's YOUR job. Use Planning Mode as your hype-helper, not your replacement. Combine it with classic builder plugins like F3X, Archimedes, and GapFill and you're gonna cook.

Go try Planning Mode today and tell me what you made. I'm building a parkour obby with floating jellyfish platforms. No notes. 🪼

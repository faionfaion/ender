# Roblox Just Smashed Fortnite's Record THREE Times — Here's How!

Okay friends, buckle up because this is absolutely WILD. Remember when Fortnite hit 15.3 million players at once back in 2020 and everyone said nothing would ever beat it? Yeah, about that...

Roblox games just crushed that record three times in a single year. THREE. TIMES. And one of them was built by a 16-year-old. I'm not even kidding.

## The Record Breakers

It all started with **Grow a Garden**. Late June 2025, this chill farming game hit **21.6 million concurrent players** and snatched Fortnite's crown. It even got a real-deal Guinness World Record for "most concurrently played video game." Huge W.

Then in August, Grow a Garden broke its OWN record with **22.3 million players**. Just flexing at this point.

But here comes the plot twist. **Steal a Brainrot** entered the chat in September with its Extinct Event and shot up to **24.1 million players**. Then in October 2025, it did something no game has ever done — it blew past **25.4 million concurrent players**. Steal a Brainrot is literally the first video game in history to cross 25 million. Nuts.

## A 16-Year-Old Made The First Record Game

This part is my favorite. Grow a Garden? Built by a 16-year-old in just a few days. Released March 26, 2025, and by summer it was bigger than half of Steam combined. If that doesn't make you want to open Roblox Studio right now, idk what will.

## The Whole Platform Went Crazy Too

When Steal a Brainrot was popping off, the entire Roblox platform peaked at **47.29 million people playing at once**. That's the biggest gaming platform moment ever recorded. Ever. Your whole city, multiplied, all playing Roblox at the same time.

## All-Time Visit Leaderboard

For the stat nerds (me, I'm the stat nerd), here's the visits leaderboard:

- Brookhaven RP — 69+ billion visits
- Blox Fruits — 52.9 billion
- Adopt Me! — 40 billion
- Grow a Garden — 21+ billion (fastest climb EVER)

Grow a Garden hitting 21 billion visits that fast is unheard of. Most games take years to reach numbers like that.

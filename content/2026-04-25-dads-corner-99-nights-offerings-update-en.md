## Hey Kiddos, Dad Here

FaionEnder reporting in from the campfire. You know I love **99 Nights in the Forest** more than I love my coffee mug collection — and that's saying something. Faion finally let me borrow the keyboard, so let's talk about the brand-new **Offerings Update** that dropped on Saturday, April 11, 2026.

This one runs through April 18, and trust your old man — it changes how every single run feels. In the best way.

## What Even Are Offerings?

Okay, picture this. You and your friends spawn in. The 9-foot Deer is somewhere out there in the trees being all spooky. Before, you mostly hoped the RNG gods were nice that night.

Now? You bring **Offerings** with you. You earn them from daily quests or buy them off the **Sketchy Salesman** NPC (great name, by the way — sounds like the guy who sold me a "like-new" car in 2003). Offerings let you trigger specific events on demand. No more pure luck — actual planning.

## The Flame System Got a Glow-Up

Here's where dad-brain lights up. **Modifier Flames** used to be locked in the lobby. You picked one before the match and that was it. Back in MY day, son, you committed to a flame and you LIVED with it.

Now you can activate Modifier Flames **in-game on Day 1**, and — get this — you can **layer them** for combos. Stack a defense flame with a resource flame. Mix a stealth flame with a healing one. It's like seasoning a stew. You don't dump the whole spice rack in; you taste, you adjust, you think ahead.

## Why This Is Secretly a Life Lesson

I'm not gonna lie — I love this update because it rewards the thing I've been telling Faion since she was 6: **slow down and plan**. The squads winning Night 50+ runs aren't the ones rushing the Deer with a spoon. They're the teams that:

- Assign roles (one gatherer, one builder, one hunter — like chores, but fun)
- Save Offerings for the rough nights instead of blowing them on Night 2
- Never. Let. The. Fire. Die. *(Dad-joke alert: keeping the home fires burning has never been so literal.)*

## Dad's Day-1 Flame Strategy

If you're jumping in this week, here's what your favorite embarrassing dad recommends:

1. Don't activate flames the second you spawn. Scout first. See what biome you got — Jungle Biome from the March 1 patch hits different.
2. Save at least one Offering for **Night 11**, when the Deer stops watching and starts stalking your campfires.
3. Talk to your team. Yes, with your actual voice. I know, I know — radical.

## The Verdict

The Offerings Update turns 99 Nights from a panic-survival game into a **strategy-survival game**, and that's a huge win. It's the kind of patch that makes me text Faion at 9 PM going "one more run?" and then we play three more.

Now if you'll excuse me, I have a Sketchy Salesman to haggle with. Stay warm out there, kiddos. 🔥

## Steal a Brainrot just made history

This game by SpyderSammy pulled **over 25 million players online at the same time**. That's the highest concurrent player count in Roblox history. EVER. Let that sink in — 25 million people, all logged in together, stealing weird little Italian cartoon animals from each other's bases.

If you haven't played, the vibe is simple: you buy Brainrot characters (those AI-voiced guys like Tralalero Tralala and Bombardiro Crocodilo from TikTok), then players try to yoink the rare ones from you. Clips of people losing their legendary brainrots are going absolutely viral. It's brutal. It's hilarious. 10/10.

## Grow a Garden is the chillest hit of the year

On the totally opposite vibe — Grow a Garden just vibes. You plant seeds, you grow crops, you touch grass (digitally). Sounds boring? It has **21 BILLION visits** and became the fastest Roblox game ever to hit 1 billion visits — in just **33 days**. Thirty. Three.

At one giant event it peaked at 8.9 million concurrent players. A farming game. Doing numbers like that. Roblox is wild right now.

## Plants vs Brainrots — the mashup nobody asked for but everybody needed

Imagine if Grow a Garden and Steal a Brainrot had a baby, and that baby also played Plants vs Zombies. That's Plants vs Brainrots. It hit **9 million concurrent players** and honestly? The tower defense chaos with meme characters yelling Italian nonsense is peak Roblox energy.

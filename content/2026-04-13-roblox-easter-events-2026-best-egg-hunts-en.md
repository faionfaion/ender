## Roblox Skipped the Big Egg Hunt — But Players Are Feasting Anyway

Okay so here's the tea — Roblox did NOT drop an official platform-wide Easter event this year. The last big one was "The Hatch" back in mid-2025, and a lot of us were wondering if they'd bring something new. Spoiler: they didn't.

But honestly? It doesn't even matter. Because over **25 individual games** are running their own Easter and spring events right now, and some of them are seriously stacked with rewards. Let me break down the best ones you can still jump into TODAY.

## Brookhaven RP — 45+ Eggs to Find

Brookhaven went absolutely crazy this year. There are **over 45 Easter eggs** hidden across the entire map, split into four difficulty tiers. Collect them to earn Bunny Tokens, then trade those in for exclusive rewards like the Spring Shuffler vehicle, an Easter Truck, a full Easter House, and a bunch of themed props. This is easily the biggest egg hunt on the platform right now.

Fun fact: Brookhaven also just became the **first game ever to hit 80 billion visits** back in March. Legends only.

## Dandy's World — Spring Skins Are Chef's Kiss

Dandy's World started their event way back on March 20 and it runs all the way through **April 24**. They introduced "Baskets" — a limited-time currency you earn just by playing. Spend them at Bassie's Store to grab exclusive Spring-Time skins. If you haven't checked this one out yet, you still have time but don't sleep on it!

## More Egg Hunts Worth Your Time

**Dress to Impress** has **30 hidden Easter eggs** scattered around — perfect if you love exploring while looking fabulous. **Bedwars** is hiding **18 eggs** throughout its maps, and **Wanted** also has **18 eggs** to track down.

Want even more time? **Horse Life** runs their spring event all the way through **May 2**, and **Epic Minigames** keeps things going until **April 27**.

## One Thing to Know

Since these are all run by individual developers and not Roblox itself, there are **no free UGC avatar items** this time around. All the rewards are in-game only for each title. A little disappointing if you were hoping for free catalog stuff, but honestly the in-game rewards are pretty fire.

## Go Get Those Eggs!

So yeah, even without an official Roblox egg hunt, this Easter season is packed. I've been bouncing between Brookhaven and Dandy's World all week and I'm having a blast. Grab your friends, pick a game, and start hunting — some of these events end soon!

Happy Easter, gamers! 🐰

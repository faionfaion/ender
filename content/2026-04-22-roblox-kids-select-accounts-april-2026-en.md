# Roblox Just Dropped HUGE News: Kids & Select Accounts Are Coming!

Okay bestie, grab your Robux and sit down because Roblox announced something MASSIVE on April 13, and it's going to change how a lot of us play. Two brand-new account types are rolling out in early June 2026, and honestly? They're kinda cool.

## Meet Roblox Kids and Roblox Select

If you're between **5 and 8 years old**, you'll get a **Roblox Kids** account. It only shows games rated Minimal or Mild, chat is off by default, and your app even has a different background color so parents know what's up.

If you're **9 to 15**, that's **Roblox Select** — you can jump into Moderate-rated games too, and chat works like normal. The best part? You auto-level-up! Kids accounts switch to Select at 9, and Select switches to the standard Roblox experience at 16. No pressing buttons, it just happens.

Games also have to pass a three-step check to even show up on under-16 accounts. So yeah, it's basically Roblox saying "we got you."

## Roblox Plus Is Actually Kinda Elite

Starting **April 30**, there's a new subscription called **Roblox Plus** for **$4.99 a month**. It gives you a **10–20% discount** on in-game items, avatar stuff, AND Robux purchases. Roblox pays the discount themselves, so creators still get their full cut. W move, honestly.

Oh — and you can now pay for the subscription with Robux. Wild.

## Avatar Makeup Just Went Live

For my fashion girlies in Dress to Impress (78.2K concurrent players and still eating), April 8 dropped the **Avatar Makeup** category with **150+ items** on day one — eyeshadow, lipstick, lashes, layered glam effects, all of it. It's the first new avatar category to launch with UGC creators ready to go from minute one. Expect your avatar to get a serious glow-up.

## Quick Hits for the Devs

If you build games, the **Animation Graph System** is in beta (way less Luau scripting!), the **Character Controller Library** is fully released, and the **Universal Importer** now handles images, audio, and video. Plus two new programs — Incubator and Jumpstart — are mentoring new creators.

So yeah, TL;DR: account glow-up, makeup glow-up, discount glow-up. Roblox is cooking. See you in-game! 💜

Okay guys, April 2026 might be the most STACKED month in Roblox history. I'm not even exaggerating. So much is happening right now that I had to sit down and tell you everything at once. Let's gooo!

## "Friends" and "Games" Are Officially Back

Remember when Roblox renamed Games to "Experiences"? Yeah, nobody liked that. On April 7, Roblox finally listened to the community and changed everything back! Your games are called Games again, and they also brought back the word Friends across the platform. Simple.

But here's the cool part — they also added a brand-new feature called Trusted Friends. It's basically your closest crew, your besties. Trusted Friends can chat and play together more freely, even if they're different ages. Super handy if you play with your siblings or cousins!

## Roblox Plus Drops April 30

Say goodbye to Roblox Premium, because Roblox Plus is taking over on April 30! It costs $4.99 a month and comes with some seriously upgraded perks. You get a 10% discount on avatar items and in-game stuff, and that goes up to 20% if you stay subscribed for 3 months straight.

Plus members also get free private servers, zero-fee Robux transfers, and access to trading. There are optional bundles too — Plus 500, Plus 1000, and Plus 2000 — that include extra Robux on top. If you already have Premium, don't worry. You keep your current plan and even get a free 1-month trial of Plus. Pretty sweet deal!

## DOORS Went Full Chaos for April Fools

DOORS absolutely cooked with their 2026 April Fools event called Rush Mode. The entire Hotel was covered in Rush faces with creepy purple lighting. Everything was cranked to max — you moved faster, Seek was faster, and items dropped like crazy. The wildest part? You could actually SHOOT entities like Rush, Ambush, and Blitz with Gold Blasters to make them vanish. They also dropped Endless Mode alongside it. Both modes are now in the Visions tab if you missed out.

## Brookhaven Broke a World Record

Oh, and did I mention that Brookhaven RP just hit 80 BILLION visits back on March 16? That's almost 10 times the entire world's population. It's the first Roblox game ever to reach that number, and honestly, it might be a while before any other game catches up.

## What's Next?

Roblox is heading to Gamescom Latam in São Paulo from April 30 to May 3, and RDC 2026 is confirmed for September in San Jose. This month is absolutely CRACKED.

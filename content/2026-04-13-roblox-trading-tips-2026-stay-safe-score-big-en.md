Hey friends! So yesterday my buddy almost got scammed out of his Dominus of the First Order — and honestly, it scared me enough to put together this quick guide. Trading in Roblox is SO fun right now, but you gotta know the rules to keep your inventory safe!

## UGC Limiteds Are Everywhere Now

If you haven't noticed, UGC Limiteds have completely taken over the trading scene in 2026. Creators can now drop items in limited quantities, and they work just like classic Roblox Limiteds — meaning you can trade and resell them!

Some of the hottest items right now include the Pastel Domino Crown, Dominus of the First Order, and Chronoavernus: The Crimson Helm. Just remember, UGC Limiteds have a holding period of up to 30 days before you can resell, so plan your trades wisely.

## Know What Your Stuff Is Actually Worth

Roblox shows you the Recent Average Price (RAP) on every Limited, but here's the thing — RAP can be manipulated by sneaky traders. Pro move: use Rolimons to check real market values and demand data before you agree to anything.

And for my Adopt Me traders out there — did you know that No Potion pets (no Fly or Ride) are often worth MORE than Fly Ride versions? Collectors love untouched pets. Check sites like Traderie for updated pet values every single day.

## Scams Are Getting Sneakier

Okay, this is the serious part. Scammers in 2026 are using some next-level tricks:

- **Beam links** are still the number one threat. Someone sends you a link to a "private server" or "portfolio" — but it actually steals your browser cookies and can bypass even two-step verification. NEVER click random links!
- **AI-generated YouTube videos** now make fake "Robux generator" proofs look super real. Spoiler: they steal your login info. Free Robux generators do not exist. Period.
- **Double-trade scams** where someone says "trade me first and I'll send my item after." They won't. Ever.
- **Fake middlemen** claiming to be official Roblox trade helpers. Roblox NEVER authorizes middlemen.

## My Top Safety Rules

Here's what I do every single time I trade:

1. **Turn on Two-Step Verification** using an authenticator app — not just email, the app is way safer.
2. **Only trade through the official Roblox trade window.** Never do trades through Discord or social media.
3. **Check every single detail** before hitting accept. Read it twice!
4. **If a deal sounds too good to be true — it is.** Walk away.

Oh, and one more thing — you need Roblox Premium to trade, and only Limited items can be traded. Regular catalog stuff, game passes, and clothing are a no-go.

Stay safe out there, traders! Your inventory is worth protecting. 💪

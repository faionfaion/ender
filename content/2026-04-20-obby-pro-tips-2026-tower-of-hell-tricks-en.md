# Obby Pro Tips 2026: How to Crush Tower of Hell and Win More

Hey besties, EnderFaion here! Okay so I've been obby-grinding non-stop this week and I finally have the tea on how to stop falling off every single jump. If you keep yeeting yourself into the void, this one's for you.

Spoiler: it's not a skill issue. It's a camera issue. Keep reading.

## The Obby Scene Is Absolutely Popping Right Now

Tower of Hell is still THE king of parkour obbys in 2026. Random tower every round, zero checkpoints, and you're racing everyone on the server. Pure chaos, I love it.

Mega Fun Obby now has over 2,400 levels (yes, two THOUSAND four hundred) with wild color themes. And if you want something fresh, Troll Obby is blowing up with invisible paths and obstacles that make zero sense until they do.

Oh, and check out Candy Obby by Juicy Drop, Cotton Obby, and Clicky Keyboard Tower. The ASMR keyboard one is my personal obsession rn.

## The #1 Tip Nobody Talks About: Camera

Okay listen up. Most players fail obbys because of bad camera angles, NOT bad timing. I learned this the hard way after eating pavement like 40 times in a row.

Always line up your camera with the direction you're jumping. Zoom in for tiny precision jumps, zoom out to see the whole path ahead. Fix your camera and suddenly you're hitting jumps like a speedrunner.

## Turn On Shiftlock. Right Now.

If you're on PC, press Shift (or enable it in settings on mobile/console). Shiftlock locks your camera behind your character and makes those "impossible" jumps actually doable. It's literally the cheat code that isn't a cheat.

Also: TAP your jump key, don't hold it. Holding makes your jumps inconsistent. Tapping gives you perfect height control every single time. Try it, thank me later.

## Tower of Hell Survival Mode

Two big things: use your double jump to clear big gaps or reach higher platforms, and DO NOT look at the timer. I'm serious. Watching the timer makes you panic, and panic makes you fall.

Just focus on the next jump. Only the next one. Slow and successful beats fast and faceplanted every single time.

## Last Resort Tips From Me to You

Practice daily on hard sections like Hole in the Wall, Neon, and Careful Climbing. Watch fail compilations on YouTube before tackling a new tower — you'll know the trap spots before they trap you.

And honestly? Play with friends. Racing your bestie through Escape Grandma's House or Escape Inflatable Obby hits way different than solo grinding. Multiplayer makes even easy obbys legendary.

# Dad's Corner: The 99 Nights Offerings Update Is Lit (Literally!)

Hey squad, FaionEnder here — Faion's dad, proud campfire-tender, and possibly the slowest lumberjack in any forest. My daughter told me to stop saying 'squad' but I'm keeping it. Deal with it, kiddo.

So last weekend I finally got to play the new **Offerings Update** in my favorite game, *99 Nights in the Forest*. It dropped on **April 11, 2026** and ran for a full week. Let me tell you — it changed the whole vibe around the campfire.

## What the Heck Is an Offering?

The devs rebuilt the flame system from the ground up. Instead of just tossing logs on the fire like a caveman (my usual strategy), you now use **Offerings** to unlock special flame effects.

There are **9 different offerings** in the pool. Each one costs **10 gems** and shows up in a Supply Crate. The catch? You can't pick which one you get. It's totally random.

Honestly, it reminds me of grocery shopping when Mom sends me with a vague list. You hope for milk. You come back with hot sauce and a pineapple. Mystery bag energy.

## Meet the Sketchy Salesman

There's a new NPC in the forest called the **Sketchy Salesman** (some folks call him the Shifty Salesman, which is also accurate). He's the guy who hands out your offerings.

Listen, I've met a few Sketchy Salesmen at flea markets in my time. This dude fits right in. Trust him with your gems, not your car keys.

The flame buffs you can score include combat boosts, better resource gathering, and survival perks. Faion got one that helped her chop wood faster. I got one that made our campfire glow purple. I'm calling that a win.

## Why This Game Is Actually Bonkers

Quick numbers drop, because I know you kids love numbers:

- **26+ billion** total plays
- **14.2 million** peak concurrent players (3rd all-time on Roblox)
- **170,000+** active players right now
- **7th most-played Roblox game ever** — in less than a year!

And here's the wild part: **20th Century Studios** picked up the movie rights. A real Hollywood *99 Nights in the Forest* film is actually happening. I already called dibs on the dad seat in the theater.

## Dad's Takeaway

The Offerings Update is a reminder of something I always tell Faion: **patience pays off in survival games**. You won't get the perfect flame on your first try. You might get the worst one twice in a row. That's fine. Keep grinding, keep the fire lit, and laugh when the random roll goes sideways.

Also, if you haven't tried the Jungle Biome Part 2 from March or the Easter Bunny class from April, go catch up. The game keeps getting better.

Now if you'll excuse me, I have a campfire to defend. And yes — I WILL be making a dad joke about 'lit' before the night is over.

Stay cozy, stay brave, and don't trust the Sketchy Salesman with your emergency gems.

— Dad out.

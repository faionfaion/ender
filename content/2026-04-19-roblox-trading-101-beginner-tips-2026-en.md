# Roblox Trading 101: Stuff I Wish Someone Told Me Before My First Trade

Okay friends, we NEED to talk about trading. I see so many of you jumping into Adopt Me! and Pet Sim 99 trades without knowing the basics — and then ending up sad because you got scammed out of your favorite pet. Not on my watch! Here's the beginner pack I wish I had when I started.

## How Trading Actually Works in 2026

First things first: to use Roblox's official trade window, you need **Roblox Premium** (starts at $4.99/month). And only **Limited** and **Limited U** items can be traded on the main platform — regular clothes and game passes can't.

Two rules to remember. Roblox takes a **30% fee** on any Robux you add to a trade. And the Robux you throw in can't be more than **50%** of the value of the items you're offering. Quick math before you click confirm!

## The Hottest Trading Games Right Now

**Adopt Me!** is still the queen of pet trading. Shadow Dragon, Frost Dragon, Bat Dragon, Giraffe, and Owl are the big names — and Neon or Mega versions are worth WAY more than regular. Check AdoptMeTradingValues or AMVGG before every trade.

**Pet Simulator 99** is on fire in 2026. The top-value pets right now are **Stacked Doge Noob** (RAP 69.75M), **Pop Cat** (52.80M), and **Blobenstein** (42.83M). Pro move: always **upgrade your pets before trading** — tier matters a ton. Murder Mystery 2 and Royale High also have active trading communities if you wanna branch out.

## My Golden Rules for Young Traders

Run every trade through a **Win/Fair/Loss (WFL)** calculator. Aim for **Win or Fair** only — never accept a Loss, even if it feels close. Rare event pets and discontinued items often spike in value later, so holding them is usually smart.

Check **RAP (Recent Average Price)** on Limiteds, but watch out for "Projected" items — that means someone pumped up the price on purpose and it's fake. Use Rolimons for Limiteds and Cosmic Values for PS99. And remember: **trades cannot be reversed**. Double-check before you tap that button.

## Scams I've Seen Way Too Many Friends Fall For

- **Trust Trade**: "You send first to prove trust!" Nope. They never send back. Ever.
- **Beam Links**: Someone DMs you a "GFX portfolio" or "private server" link. Clicking it can steal your account even with 2FA on.
- **Free Robux generators**: 100% a scam, 100% of the time. Zero exceptions.
- **Pick a Door / Spin the Wheel / Rename Pet** mini-games in Adopt Me!
- **Last-second item swap** — someone removes a pet right before you hit accept.

## Safety Checklist Before You Trade

Only trade inside **Roblox's official trade window**. Never on Discord, never on random websites. Turn on **2-Step Verification** today if you haven't. And if anyone claims to be Roblox staff messaging you in-game? That's fake — real staff never do that.

Start small with around **1,000 Robux** worth of stuff, track your trades, and learn as you go. Happy trading, and may all your WFLs be Wins!

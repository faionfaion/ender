# Roblox Plus Drops April 30! Plus New Kids & Select Accounts

Okay gamers, sit down because Roblox just dropped TWO huge updates and I can barely type fast enough. We're getting a brand-new subscription called **Roblox Plus**, AND a whole new system of age-based accounts. Let's break it down.

## Roblox Plus — Bye Premium, Hi Discounts

Roblox Premium is getting replaced by **Roblox Plus** on April 30, 2026. It costs $4.99 a month and honestly? The perks are kinda cracked.

You get **10% off** in-game items, avatars, and Robux purchases the second you subscribe. Stick with it for **3 months in a row** and that jumps to a **20% discount**. That's basically free drip if you were gonna spend Robux anyway.

## The Private Server Glow-Up

Here's the one that made me scream — **paid private servers are FREE and UNLIMITED** for Plus members. Yes, really. Squad up in your own private lobby of any game, no more splitting costs.

Plus members can also send **Robux to friends with zero transaction fees**, get a shiny subscriber badge on their profile, and soon there'll be bundles called **Plus 500, Plus 1000, and Plus 2000** that throw in monthly Robux on top.

## New Roblox Kids & Roblox Select Accounts

In early June 2026, Roblox is launching two new account types for younger players. **Roblox Kids** is for ages 5 to 8 — only "Minimal" or "Mild" rated games, chat turned off. **Roblox Select** is for ages 9 to 15 — games up to "Moderate" rating and limited chat.

Each account gets matched to a curated list of **thousands of approved games** that updates automatically. When you hit age 9, Kids upgrades to Select. When you hit 16, Select upgrades to a regular account. Smooth.

## What Parents Can Do

Parents get a control panel that lets them set **screen time, spending limits**, approve or block specific games, and see your friends list and most-played titles. Later in 2026, Roblox is swapping to the **IARC rating system** — that's ESRB in the US and PEGI in Europe.

## Other Cool April Stuff

Quick hits before I go: voice chat now supports **up to 100 people** in a call (chaotic in the best way), **Avatar Makeup** just hit the Marketplace, the **Animation Graph System beta** is live for builders, and the Roblox app now runs in the background so you never miss a game invite.

Roblox also announced it's hitting **13 billion hours** of monthly playtime. We are literally never logging off.

So mark your calendar — April 30 is Plus day. See you in a private server, bestie!

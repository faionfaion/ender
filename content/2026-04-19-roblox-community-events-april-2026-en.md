# Roblox Community Events Are POPPING Off in April 2026!

Okay squad, you will NOT believe how much is going on in the Roblox community this month. Like, I literally had to sit down and make a list because my brain was spinning. From fan-made expos to massive streamer events, April 2026 is stacked.

And the best part? Most of this stuff was built BY the community, FOR the community. That is so cool.

## PEC26 April Expo — Theme Parks Take Over

The Park Expo Collective just wrapped their **PEC26 April Expo** (April 4–5), and it was honestly one of the coolest fan-run events I have ever seen inside Roblox. It ran from 2:00 PM April 4 to 6:00 PM April 5 GMT, and anyone could walk in.

You could visit booths, ride upcoming attractions, watch live showcases of brand-new 2026 park projects, and even sit in on real conference talks. The **PEC26 RTPR April Awards** went down April 5 at 7:00 PM GMT and crowned the top theme park builders. Solo creators, small crews, huge studios — everyone was welcome.

## Bloxfest 2026 Flashback — That $300K Prize Pool

Earlier this year, Twitch dropped **Bloxfest 2026** (Feb 5–27) and it was WILD. We are talking a **$300,000 prize pool** and a "Clash of the Couches" charity event where top Twitch streamers teamed up with NFL players for a $50,000 charity bag.

If you tuned in on Twitch, you could unlock limited-time in-game items from real Roblox developers. Drops like that are why you always, always keep the stream open.

## What's Coming Next

Mark your calendars because the hype is not slowing down:

- **Roblox Developer Challenge 2026** — a 72-hour game jam where creators race to build themed experiences. If you love making games, this one is pure chaos (the good kind).
- **Gamescom Latam 2026** — Roblox heads to São Paulo, Brazil from **April 30 to May 3** with creator challenges and meetups.
- **RDC26** — the Roblox Developers Conference returns to **San Jose, California, September 10–12**, with the Roblox Innovation Awards handed out to the best creators on the whole platform.

## Other April Buzz

A couple more things worth knowing: on **April 7**, Roblox renamed "Connections" back to **Friends** (thank goodness, we all missed the old name). Then on **April 10**, they announced **Roblox Plus**, a new subscription that replaces Premium starting **April 30**. Games ran their own stuff too — shoutout to the DOORS April Fools "Rush Mode" from April 1–6 for scaring everyone silly.

## Why This Matters

The biggest takeaway? You do not have to work at Roblox HQ to host an event. Kids, teens, solo builders — anyone can put something together and the community will show up. That is the magic of this platform. Go check out the Roblox Creator Events portal and see what's next — I'll see you there!

## Free /e Commands You Might Be Missing

Did you know you've had free emotes this whole time? Just open the chat and type /e followed by the emote name. Try /e dance, /e dance2, or /e dance3 for some classic moves. But there's more! Type /e wave, /e laugh, /e cheer, or /e point to show off in any server.

These all work on any R15 avatar, so literally everyone can do them right now. Go try it, I'll wait!

## UGC Emotes Changed Everything

Here's the biggest deal — Roblox recently opened up emote creation to approved UGC designers. That means regular creators (not just Roblox themselves) can now make and sell custom dances and animations on the Marketplace.

The result? An absolute explosion of creativity. Tons of trending Marketplace items right now are emotes, and new ones get published every single day. Your avatar isn't just about what you wear anymore — it's about how you MOVE.

## The Emote Movement Trick

Okay, this one is super fun to try. In some games, you can use emotes to pull off cool movement tricks! By quickly switching between actions and triggering an emote, you can make your character glide or slide around in unexpected ways. Pair it with sprinting and it looks hilarious — like your avatar is moonwalking through the server. It takes practice, but once you nail it? SO satisfying.

## Emote RNG Is Blowing Up

If you haven't tried Emote RNG yet, what are you doing?! This game has you rolling for random dance animations — from common wiggles all the way up to ultra-rare ones with wild visual effects. Earn cash, upgrade your luck, and try to collect the rarest emotes possible. New codes drop regularly too!

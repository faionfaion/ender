## A Teen, a Garden, and 22 Million Players

Okay besties, buckle up because the Roblox record book basically caught FIRE recently and I need to tell you everything!

Let's start with the most insane story. A 16-year-old from New Zealand — username BMWLux — built a little gardening game called Grow a Garden in just a few days. You buy seeds, grow crops, earn coins. Simple, right? WRONG. This game hit 22.3 million players online AT THE SAME TIME on August 23, 2025. That's the highest concurrent player count for any single experience in Roblox history — and one of the biggest peaks ever recorded for ANY game. For context, that number rivals the kind of player counts major live events in games like Fortnite pull off!

Oh, and it reached 1 billion visits in just 33 days. THIRTY-THREE DAYS. The game now has over 32 billion total visits. A teenager made this. Let that sink in.

## Brookhaven Hits 80 BILLION Visits

While Grow a Garden was speedrunning records, Brookhaven RP just kept doing its thing. On March 16, 2026, it became the first Roblox game EVER to reach 80 billion visits. That's roughly 10 times the entire population of Earth. Brookhaven is still the most-visited experience in all of Roblox history, and honestly, it doesn't look like anyone's catching up anytime soon.

Blox Fruits is putting up a fight though — it just hit 60 billion visits on March 23, making it only the third game to reach that milestone!

## A Player Beat Cheaters to Win a Guinness World Record

Here's my favorite story. Guinness World Records held their first-ever official record attempt ON Roblox back in November 2025 with a challenge called the 1% Win Obby. An 18-year-old player named Quinten Delaere (kannotlogin) noticed that players above him on the leaderboard had suspiciously fake times. So what did he do? He used data analysis to prove they were cheating!

Guinness investigated, voided all the fake entries, and crowned Quinten the real winner. Legend behavior honestly.

## Roblox Is Absolutely Massive Right Now

The platform itself is breaking records too. Roblox now has around 380 million monthly active users worldwide and about 111.8 million people play every single day. The peak number of players online across ALL of Roblox hit 47.4 million. Those numbers are wild.

Also fun fact — the Builderman account turned 20 years old on March 8, 2026. Happy birthday to the OG!

The biggest takeaway from all of this? You don't need a huge team or a massive budget to make something incredible. A kid from New Zealand proved that with some seeds and a garden. Now go make YOUR dream game!

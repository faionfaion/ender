# Obby Pro Secrets 2026: The Tips Every Roblox Fan Needs RN

OK so I just spent like a whole weekend grinding obbies and I HAVE to share what I learned. If you've been rage-quitting on stage 5 of Tower of Hell, this one's for you bestie!

Obbies are having a MASSIVE moment in 2026. New UGC reward obbies are dropping every single week, and the free avatar items you can grab by finishing them are way better than they were last year. Like, actually wearable, not just weird hats.

## The Trending Obbies You Gotta Try

Tower of Hell by YXceptional Studios is still the king. It's been around since June 2018 and in 2026 it's got 330 regular stages plus 32 secret ones. Every 8 minutes the game picks 6 towers from a pool of 362 — so no two rounds feel the same. No checkpoints though, so bring patience.

If Tower of Hell sounds scary (fair), start with Mega Easy Obby. Tons of checkpoints, chill difficulty, perfect for warm-ups. Mega Fun Obby has 2,400+ levels if you want something HUGE, and 500 Levels Obby is great because the difficulty creeps up slowly so you don't get slapped.

Also peep Obby Tower (billions of visits, wall slides are so satisfying), Escape Grandma's House, and Ninja Obby where you actually get ninja moves built into the gameplay. Cool, right?

## Pro Tips That Actually Work

Tip one, and this is the BIGGEST: turn off shift lock unless you really need it. Free camera makes platforming so much easier, trust me. I was stuck for weeks until I tried this.

Tip two: drop your graphics settings. Frame drops are literally the number one reason people miss jumps. Smooth game = smooth jumps.

Tip three: edge jumping. Walk to the very edge of a block before you jump — you get WAY more distance. Paired with the mini-jump (tap jump instead of holding it), you can hit tiny platforms like a pro.

Tip four: chain your jumps to keep momentum. Stopping every 5 seconds is what kills most Tower of Hell runs. Speedrunners on YouTube do this constantly and it's why they look like they're gliding.

Tip five: look for hidden checkpoints! Lots of obbies hide glowing pads or little orbs near the hardest sections. Devs are nicer than you think.

## The Real Secret

Stay calm. I know, I know — but frustration literally ruins your timing. Deep breath, reset, try again. Beginners who practice even 15 minutes a day can clear advanced obbies in a few weeks. That's not cap, that's just how muscle memory works.

Now go claim some free UGC items and flex them in the lobby. You got this!

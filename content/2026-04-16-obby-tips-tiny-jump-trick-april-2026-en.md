Hey besties, it's EnderFaion, and I have to spill the tea on something that literally changed my obby life. For YEARS I was holding the jump button like my finger was glued to it — and dying on the same spinning platform over and over. Then I learned the mini-jump, and now I'm vibing through Tower of Hell like it's a chill Sunday stroll. Let me hook you up with the best tips for April 2026!

## Stop Holding Jump — Start Tapping

Here's the #1 glow-up: TAP the jump button instead of holding it. Holding gives you a full-height leap, which is exactly why you keep overshooting those tiny platforms.

Mini-jumps (just a quick tap) give you short, precise hops. Use them on small ledges, narrow beams, and those evil one-stud platforms in Tower of Hell. Trust me, this one switch feels like getting a cheat code.

## The Shift Lock Question

Everyone asks me about Shift Lock, so let's settle it. Go to Settings and turn Shift Lock Switch to ON so you HAVE the option. But don't use it everywhere!

Shift Lock is amazing for angled jumps and first-person precision spots. For normal platforming though? Free camera is way easier. Flip it on only when you need that laser-aim vibe.

## Flow Like Water

Newbies pause after every single jump. Pros keep moving. Momentum is your bestie in obbies — try to chain jumps together without stopping between platforms.

Also, angle your camera to match where you're going. Side views help with timed traps, and you can even tilt the camera mid-jump to nudge your landing. Sneaky but legal!

## Pick the Right Obby for Your Level

Don't yeet yourself straight into Tower of Hell (330 main stages, 8-minute timer, zero checkpoints — brutal). Level up first with these:

- **500 Levels** — super gentle curve, perfect for starters
- **Mega Fun Obby** — 2,400+ themed levels, endless fun
- **Escape Grandma's House** and **Chill Obby** — cozy kid-friendly vibes
- **Obby Tower** — when you're ready to sweat

And here's a bonus: the top 15 UGC obbies of 2026 are literally GIVING AWAY free avatar items just for finishing stages. Free drip? Yes please.

## The Boring Tip That Saves Lives

Lower your graphics settings. I know, I know — but frame drops are the #1 reason you're randomly dying on jumps you nailed five times before. Smooth frames = smooth jumps.

Also, watch a walkthrough before a hard section. It's not cheating, it's studying. See you at the top, legends!

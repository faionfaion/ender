# Roblox Studio 2026: AI Helper Writes Your Game Code Now!

Okay besties, you HAVE to hear this. Roblox Studio just got the biggest glow-up ever, and honestly? Building your own game has never been this easy. I've been testing the new stuff all week and I'm low-key obsessed.

If you've ever wanted to make your own obby or tycoon but got scared of coding — this news is literally for you.

## The New Studio UI Is So Clean

First up, the whole Studio interface got redesigned late last year and finished rolling out in 2026. It's got this shiny new "Liquid Glass" look and a flexible layout that actually makes sense.

The Top Ribbon now has five simple tabs: Home, Model, View, Test, and Plugins. All the Play and Test buttons live in one spot, so you don't have to hunt around. There's even a new Viewport menu that helps you fix bugs when your game goes sideways.

## Assistant Just Leveled Up Big Time

On February 25, 2026, Roblox dropped a massive update to the Studio Assistant — and this is where it gets wild. Code Assist is now fully released, which means AI can auto-complete your Luau scripts while you type. It's like having a super-smart friend helping you code!

There's also something called Developer Intelligence — an AI trained ONLY on Roblox stuff. So when you ask it a question, you get real Roblox answers, not random internet nonsense.

And the craziest part? Playtest Automation. The AI can literally test your game for you. No more begging your friends to join just to find bugs.

## Mobile Game Builders, Rejoice

Here's a feature I didn't know I needed: multi-touch gesture simulation. You can now fake pinches, rotations, and two-finger pans using just your mouse while playtesting. Perfect for anyone making a mobile Roblox game.

## Where To Start If You're New

The Roblox Creator Hub has free curriculum paths built just for beginners. My top picks:

- Intro to World Building
- ModuleScript Basics
- Make Your First Clothing
- Code with Assistant (the new AI learning path)

Pro tip from me to you: start with an Obby template. It's the easiest first project, and you'll have something playable super fast. Expect about 20–40 hours of practice before you're building real game mechanics in Luau — but with AI helping, that curve is way less scary.

## Plugins Worth Trying

If you want extra AI power, check out RoCode v2.0 — it pops an AI coding helper right next to your script window. There's also RobloxAIBuilder, which combines coding AND building help in one plugin.

Honestly, 2026 is THE year to start making your own Roblox games. The tools are easier, the AI has your back, and the tutorials are totally free. Go build something epic — I wanna play it!

Hey besties, EnderFaion here! I've been grinding Roblox non-stop this month, and I NEED to tell you which games are absolutely blowing up right now. Like, we're talking hundreds of thousands of players online at the same time. Let's gooo!

## Grow a Garden — The Cozy Game Taking Over Everything

Okay, if you haven't played Grow a Garden yet, what are you even doing? This cute little farming sim just passed **21 BILLION visits** — that's billion with a B. It got to 1 billion visits faster than almost any Roblox game ever. Wild.

You plant seeds, grow crops, collect pets, and the best part? You literally only need like 5 minutes a day to keep your farm popping. It works perfectly on phones too, which is clutch for playing between classes.

## Easter Event 2026 Part 2 Is LIVE

The Easter Event Part 2 dropped on April 12, and it's stacked. There's a brand-new **Season 4 Pass** with 50 levels of rewards, including an exclusive Easter Garden plot, new seeds, cute pets, and gear you can't get anywhere else.

Pro tip: the devs drop patch notes every Saturday with new crops and mutations, so check in on weekends! They also said the game is entering a "new era" with deeper farming coming soon. Big moves.

## 99 Nights in the Forest — For My Spooky Kids

If cozy isn't your vibe, 99 Nights in the Forest is THE game right now — **442,000 players** online at the same time. That's insane. You have to survive 99 days in a creepy forest, defend your campfire, and rescue 4 missing kids while dodging this freaky deer creature and some cultists. It's spooky but not gory, so it's totally fine.

There are **20+ character classes** to unlock, so you can really find a playstyle that fits you. The new **Jungle Biome** dropped March 1, and the Easter update on March 28 added egg hunting and puzzles. So much to do!

## What About the Classics?

Don't worry, the OGs are still going strong. **Brookhaven RP** is STILL number one with 665K concurrent players and 78 billion total visits. **Adopt Me** is pulling 531K, and **Dress to Impress** (my personal fashion obsession) is crushing it at 188K.

## My Honest Take

2026 is all about two vibes: chill cozy games and deep adventure worlds. Grow a Garden and 99 Nights are leading the charge, but honestly? Just play what makes you happy. That's the real W.

Catch ya in the garden (or the forest)! 🌱

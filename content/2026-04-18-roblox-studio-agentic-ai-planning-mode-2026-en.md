# Roblox Studio Just Got an AI Brain — Build Games by Just Talking!

Okay, you need to sit down for this one. On April 16, 2026, Roblox dropped the biggest Studio update ever — Roblox Studio is officially going **agentic**. That's a fancy word that basically means the AI Assistant can now think, plan, and build games with you like a real coding buddy. No cap, this changes EVERYTHING for young creators.

And check this stat: 44% of the top 1,000 Roblox creators are already using AI tools to build their games. Now imagine that power in YOUR hands!

## Planning Mode — Your Game Idea, Mapped Out

Here's the wildest feature. You type something like "a pirate adventure obby with moving ships" and the Assistant turns it into a full game design document. Like, step-by-step build tasks, all organized!

The best part? It asks you clarifying questions first. So if you want the pirate to have a parrot sidekick, YOU stay in charge of the vision. The AI is just there to help make your dream real.

## Procedural Models = Instant 3D Objects

Want a bookcase? Just say how many shelves you want. Need a spooky staircase? Pick the height and BOOM — it's textured and ready. The new Procedural Model Generation creates fully finished 3D stuff right from your prompt.

No more messing with individual bricks for hours. This alone can cut game build time by 80–90%. Projects that used to take weeks? Done in 1 to 6 hours for simple games. That's literally one weekend.

## The Playtesting Agent Is Actually Insane

This one blew my mind. There's a new Playtesting Agent (still in beta) that actually PLAYS your game like a QA tester would. It checks the logs, sees if things work the way you planned, and suggests fixes.

It's called a "self-correcting loop" — meaning your game basically fixes itself while you grab a snack. Wild times.

## Other Cool Studio Drops

Roblox also rolled out multi-touch gesture simulation (test pinch and rotate on mobile with just your mouse), a redesigned Place Version History with search and filters, and a new Animation Graph System beta. Plus third-party AI tools like Claude, Cursor, and Codex can now plug into Studio through the built-in MCP server. Power move.

## How to Start Building TODAY

Head to **create.roblox.com** — the Creator Hub is totally free. Start with beginner tutorials like "Intro to World Building" and "ModuleScript Basics." The coding language is called Luau and honestly it's super chill, kinda like Python.

When you get stuck, the AI Assistant explains the code and fixes errors for you. And creators like AlvinBlox and TheDevKing on YouTube make the BEST tutorial videos if you want to level up fast.

Seriously, there's never been a better time to make your first Roblox game. Your candy kingdom obby is waiting — go build it! 🎮

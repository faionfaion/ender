## Dad's Corner is IN SESSION

Hey team, FaionEnder here — dad, gamer, and professional campfire-watcher. Pull up a log by the fire, because I have TWO huge things to tell you, and one of them might mean you're stuck with me in voice chat forever. You've been warned.

## I am now officially a Trusted Friend

On April 2, 2026, Roblox rolled out the expanded Trusted Friends system, and here's the wild part — if a parent has a linked Roblox account with their kid, they get automatically upgraded from regular Friend to Trusted Friend. No paperwork. No secret handshake. Just BAM, dad powers activated.

What does that actually mean? Trusted Friends can use Party Voice and unfiltered chat inside games, so my daughter and I can actually talk strategy while we play. Roblox still watches for serious safety stuff in the background, which is great, because parents like me were mostly asking for one thing: please let me tell my kid to stop running toward the wolves.

Parents do have to pass a quick age check and be linked up first. And here's the bonus intel — in early June 2026, Roblox is launching two new age-based accounts: Roblox Kids for ages 5 to 8, and Roblox Select for ages 9 to 15. Each one tunes content, chat, and parental controls to the right age group. Pretty cool upgrade for the whole family.

## Meanwhile, in my favorite forest

You knew I had to bring up 99 Nights in the Forest. The Easter Part 2 update dropped April 4, 2026, and it stuffed the game with two new Easter Eggs, a limited-time class, pet skins, furniture, and event currency rewards. I spent one whole evening just decorating my shelter. My daughter called it 'embarrassing.' I call it interior design.

But the big one is the Offerings Update, live from Saturday, April 11, 2026 at 12:00 PM EST through April 18 at 7:50 PM EST. It overhauls the entire flame system, making fire upgrades more dynamic and way more accessible. For a game where rule number one is KEEP THE CAMPFIRE ALIVE, that's a massive deal.

And don't forget the Jungle Biome Part 2 stuff from March 7 — Cat Entity, Jungle Fight Pits, Mossy Mammoths. The jungle is rude now. Bring friends.

## Dad wisdom for the squad

Here's my free advice, worth every cent you paid for it. Assign roles: one gatherer, one builder, one hunter. Share your loot. Revive teammates with bandages. And for the love of marshmallows — never, ever let the fire go out. Patience wins forests, kiddo. Also taxes, but we'll talk about that when you're older.

Now link those accounts and let's go survive 99 nights together. I promise to only tell three dad jokes per match.
